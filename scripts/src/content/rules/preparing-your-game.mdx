## Force Organization


The army you play a game of Alt-Hammer with is comprised of Units. There is no limitation on the total number of Units your force can include, however, your army's force organization must abide by the following: 
1. Your army must include at least 1 <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> unit. 
2. 1 <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> unit in your army must be designated as your Warlord. Note that some Factions have <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> units that must be your Warlord if you take them, as described in their Unit Profile. Under a Force Organization section.
3. You can include a maximum of 2 of the same units in your army for every 1,000 points, unless that Unit either:
4. Possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword, in which case you can take up to 2 such units for every 500 points,
5. or
6. Possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="character" data-type="keyword">Character</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="titanic" data-type="keyword">Titanic</span> keywords, in which case you can take up to 1 such unit for every 1,000 points,
7. or
8. Is from the Dedicated Transports unit category, in which case you can take 1 such unit for every <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> unit in your army,
9. or
10. Has Unit-specific conditions on the number that can be included in your army, as identified on its Unit Profile under a Force Organization section.

## Pregame Setup


Alt-Hammer games begin by determining the mission that will be played according to the **Generating a Battle** section, or by another mission design strategy agreed to by the players.

Once the Battle is generated, the following step is completed, which commences the battle:

**Determine Player Initiative for Battle Round 1:** Each player rolls a D6. The player with the highest roll takes the Initiative for the first Battle Round. Having the Initiative determines the order that the following items are completed, from highest Initiative to lowest:
1. Pre-battle Rules that apply to a player's army (ie <span class="keyword" data-term="scout-x" data-type="keyword">Scout [X]"</span> moves)
2. The Command Phase
3. The first unit Activation in Battle Round 1 

Please note that Initiative is determined separately for each Battle Round, as detailed in Phase 3 of The Battle Round.
