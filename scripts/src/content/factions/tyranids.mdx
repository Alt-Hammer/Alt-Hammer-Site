## Army Rules

##### Synapse Control {#synapse-control}

Several Tyranids units possess the Synapse [X]” keyword, allowing these units to control and enhance the resilience and use of lesser bioforms more common to a Tyranids army. The Synapse [X]” keyword confers the following ability:

###### Synapse [X]” {#synapse-x}

This unit provides the following benefits to all Tyranids Beast units with at least one model within [X]” of this unit:
- Increase the Activation Points of the unit by 1
- Gain the Fearless ability

##### Shadow in the Warp {#shadow-in-the-warp}

Enemy Psykers must take a Battle Shock Test at the start of their Activation when they are wholly within [X]” of at least 1 Tyranid unit with the Synapse [X]” ability. If the Battle Shock Test is failed, that enemy Psyker is Battle Shocked.


## Detachment Traits

Tyranid armies are made up of Detachment Traits that reflect unique evolutions common to known Hive Fleets and Splinter Fleets identified across the galaxy. Your Tyranid army has 2 Detachment Trait Points that you may ‘spend’ when constructing your army list by selecting Detachment Traits from the following options. 

###### Endless Swarm	2 Detachment Points {#endless-swarm-2-detachment-points}

- Each time a Tyranid Battleline Beast unit from your army is Activated, if that unit is within range of a unit with the Synapse [X]” ability, you can immediately activate 1 other Battleline unit of the same type on the battlefield. Both of these units count as a single Activation, but must complete the same Actions at the same time.
- Each time a Tyranid Battleline Beast model from your army is destroyed, roll 1 D6. On a 5+ place that model into a group off of the table. This is your next Endless Swarm Wave. At the start of the next Battle Round, organize the models in your Endless Swarm Wave into units of matching models - 1 unit per model type (ie, 1 unit of Termagaunts, 1 unit of Hormagaunts), which are then considered to be in Strategic Reserves. These units must then be Activated (following the standard rules for activating units in Strategic Reserves) and must be placed anywhere on the battlefield so that it is wholly within 6” of any Battlefield Edge in your Deployment Zone.

###### Vanguard Invaders	1 Detachment Point {#vanguard-invaders-1-detachment-point}

The following units gain the Battleline keyword: Genestealers, Lictor
The following units lose the Battleline keyword: Termagaunts, Hormagaunts, Warriors

###### Crusher Stampede	1 Detachment Point {#crusher-stampede-1-detachment-point}

The following units gain the Battleline keyword: Carnifexes, Maleceptor 
The following units lose the Battleline keyword: Termagaunts, Hormagaunts, Warriors

###### Aerial Scourge	1 Detachment Point {#aerial-scourge-1-detachment-point}

The following units gain the Battleline keyword: Gargoyles, Harpy
The following units lose the Battleline keyword: Termagaunts, Hormagaunts, Warriors

Models equipped with a Warp Field gain the Invulnerable Save 5+ ability. Additionally, once per Battle, models equipped with a Warp Field can attempt to enhance their Warp Field. To do so, take a Psychic Test. If the test is passed, the bearer gains the Invulnerable Save 4+ ability until the end of the Battle Round.

###### Twin-Linked Weapon	Varies (see below)	 {#twin-linked-weapon-varies-see-below}

Weapon gains the Twin-linked ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 
Twin-linked weapons select 1 of 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.
