## Army Rules

##### Cult Ambush

Every Genestealer Cults Infantry unit possesses the Deep Strike ability. Additionally, a Genestealer Cults army ignores the limitations on the number of units that can be placed in Strategic Reserves before the start of the battle. However, at least 2 Genestealer Cults Infantry must Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves during each Battle Round.

##### The Time is Nigh

Every Genestealer Cults Infantry unit that uses the Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves Action, either via the Deep Strike ability or via a table edge, follows the following rules for deploying to the battlefield in lieu of the typical rules for doing so:
- Units must be placed such that every model is within 3" of a Terrain Feature.
- Units must be placed on the battlefield such that no model is within the following distances of any enemy models:
- 12" in Battle Round 1
- 8" in Battle Round 2
- 6" in Battle Round 3
- 4" in Battle Round 4
- 2" in Battle Round 5

##### Return to the Shadows

Genestealer Cults Infantry units have access to the following Action:

###### Return to the Shadows	2 Activation Points

A Genestealer Cults Infantry unit can use the following Action if it has:
- already completed either a Move or Shoot Action as part of its Activation, but no other Actions,
- when the unit is wholly within 3" of a Terrain Feature, and,
- is more than 6" away from any enemy models. 

When it does so, remove the unit from the table and place it into Strategic Reserves. 

##### Broodcoven (Force Organization)

Only 1 of each Genestealer Cults Character unit can be included in a Genestealer Cults army.

##### Brood Brothers

Up to 25% of your Genestealer Cults army can be comprised of the following Astra Militarum units:

When including these units, they gain the Genestealer Cults keyword, but do not benefit from this faction's Army Rules, and cannot make selections from Astra Militarum Detachment Traits. However, you gain the ability to use the **Voice of Command** and **Regimental Coordination Army** Rules available to the Astra Militarum faction with these units.
