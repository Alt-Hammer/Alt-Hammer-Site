## Army Rules

##### Cult Ambush


Every <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults-infantry" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults Infantry </span>unit possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability. Additionally, a Genestealer Cults army ignores the limitations on the number of units that can be placed in Strategic Reserves before the start of the battle. However, at least 2 <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults-infantry" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults Infantry </span>must <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> during each Battle Round.


##### The Time is Nigh


Every <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults-infantry" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults Infantry </span>unit that uses the <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> Action, either via the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability or via a table edge, follows the following rules for deploying to the battlefield in lieu of the typical rules for doing so:

- Units must be placed such that every model is within 3" of a Terrain Feature.
- Units must be placed on the battlefield such that no model is within the following distances of any enemy models:
- 12" in Battle Round 1
- 8" in Battle Round 2
- 6" in Battle Round 3
- 4" in Battle Round 4
- 2" in Battle Round 5

##### Return to the Shadows


<span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults-infantry" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults Infantry </span>units have access to the <span class="keyword" data-term="return-to-the-shadows" data-type="action">Return to the Shadows</span> Action.


##### Broodcoven (Force Organization)


Only 1 of each <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults-character" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults Character</span> unit can be included in a Genestealer Cults army.


##### Brood Brothers


Up to 25% of your <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults</span> army can be comprised of the following <span class="keyword" data-term="astra-militarum" data-type="keyword">Astra Militarum</span> units:

- **Characters**
- Senior Officer
- Commissar
- Ogryn Bodyguard

**Battleline**
- Infantry Section

**Infantry**
- Command Squad
- Special Weapons Section

**Dedicated Transports**
- Chimera
- Taurox

**Walkers**
- Sentinels

**Vehicles**
- Light Artillery Teams
- Heavy Artillery Teams
- Onager Self Propelled Artillery
- Chimera Self Propelled Artillery
- Hellhound Light Assault Tank
- Leman Russ Battle Tank
- Rogal Dorn Heavy Battle Tank
- Baneblade Super-Heavy Battle Tank
- Stormlord Super-Heavy Assault Transport
- Shadowsword Super-Heavy Tank Hunter

When including these units, they gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults</span> keyword, but do not benefit from this faction's Army Rules, and cannot make selections from Astra Militarum Detachment Traits. However, you gain the ability to use the **Voice of Command** and **Regimental Coordination **Army Rules available to the Astra Militarum faction with these units.


## Wargear Upgrades

<h6 id="master-crafted">Master-crafted<span class="heading-value">10 points per weapon</span></h6>

- Increase weapon's Attacks characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon's Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon's Damage characteristic by 1

<h6 id="psychic-familiar">Psychic Familiar<span class="heading-value">10 points</span></h6>


During your Command Phase, the bearer can use a Psychic Familiar. If it does, until the end of the Battle Round, add 6" to the range of 1 of its **[Psychic]** abilities, or 1 of its Psychic attacks. When you choose to use a Psychic Familiar for this ability, roll a D6. On a 3+, destroy 1 Psychic Familiar model.


<h6 id="cult-icon">Cult Icon<span class="heading-value">30 Points</span></h6>

- Friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults-infantry" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults Infantry</span> units that can draw line of sight to the bearer, and who have at least 1 model within 12" of the bearer, increase their Leadership characteristic by 1.
- While the bearer is on the battlefield and positioned wholly within range of a Primary Objective Marker in No Man's Land, it is considered to have an Objective Control value of 20.
- While the bearer is on the battlefield and positioned wholly within range of a Primary Objective Marker in No Man's Land, friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="genestealer-cults-infantry" data-type="keyword">Genestealer Cults Infantry</span> units currently in Strategic Reserves reduce the required minimum distance they must maintain from enemy units as detailed under the Cult Ambush rule when they use the <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> Action within 12" of the bearer.

<h6 id="spotter">Spotter<span class="heading-value">8 Points</span></h6>


When the bearer begins its Activation with the <span class="keyword" data-term="remain-stationary" data-type="action">Remain Stationary</span> Action, it increases its Ballistic Skill by 1 when making Ranged attacks against an enemy unit.


<h6 id="smoke-launchers">Smoke Launchers<span class="heading-value">10 Points</span></h6>


The bearer can make 2 attacks with <span class="weapon-ref" data-weapon="smoke-grenades">Smoke Grenades</span> as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span> action.


<h6 id="survey-augur">Survey Augur<span class="heading-value">10 Points</span></h6>


The bearer ignores the effects of the Stealth ability when selecting enemy units as the target of a Ranged attack.


<h6 id="dozer-grinder">Dozer-Grinder<span class="heading-value">25 Points</span></h6>


Models equipped with a Dozer-Grinder gain the <span class="weapon-ref" data-weapon="dozer-grinder">Dozer-Grinder</span> melee weapon. Additionally, models equipped with a Dozer-Grinder improve their Breach Test roll by 1 and do not reduce their Movement characteristic by 2" when moving through a terrain feature.


<h6 id="twin-linked">Twin-linked<span class="heading-value">Varies (see below)</span></h6>


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit's datasheet. The points cost for the Twin-linked upgrade varies, as described below. 

This upgrade costs 12 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more

This upgrade costs 4 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower

The upgrade costs 8 points for all other weapons.
