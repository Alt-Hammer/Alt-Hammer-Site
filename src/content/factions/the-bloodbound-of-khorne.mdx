## Army Rules

##### Blood for the Blood God

The following are conferred on to Khorne units each time they destroy an enemy unit with a Melee attack:
- After the first enemy unit is destroyed, the unit increase their Leadership characteristic by 1
- After the second enemy unit is destroyed, the unit increases its Strength characteristic by 1
- After the third enemy unit is destroyed, the unit increases its Attacks characteristic by 1
- After the fourth enemy unit is destroyed, the unit increases its Weapons Skill characteristic by 1
- After the fifth enemy unit is destroyed, the unit increases its Initiative characteristic by 1
- After the sixth enemy unit is destroyed, the unit gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability
- After the seventh enemy unit is destroyed, the unit increases its Movement characteristic by 1”
- After the eighth unit is destroyed, all of the above benefits are further increased by 1.

##### Blessings of Khorne

When a friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="khorne-infantry" data-type="keyword">Khorne Infantry</span> unit has a starting strength of 8 models:
- Increase the Leadership characteristic of models in the unit by 1
- Add 2 **Blood for the Blood God** abilities each time they destroy an enemy unit in Melee, instead of 1
- Add 1 to the Objective Control value of models in that unit

##### Daemonhost

Up to 50% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the Chaos Daemons Faction, but these units must possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="khorne" data-type="keyword">Khorne</span> keyword.
