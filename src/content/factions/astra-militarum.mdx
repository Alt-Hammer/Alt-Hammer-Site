## Army Rules

##### Voice of Command


<span class="keyword" data-term="astra-militarum-leader" data-type="keyword">Astra Militarum Leader</span> models from your army with this ability can issue Orders. Each <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> Unit's Voice of Command ability will specify how many Orders it can issue and which units are eligible to receive those Orders. 

<span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> units can issue Orders under 1 of the following scenarios:
- When the <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> is on the battlefield or embarked in a <span class="keyword" data-term="transport" data-type="keyword">Transport </span>that is on the battlefield.
- Immediately after the <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader </span>uses the <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> Action.

Each time a <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> issues an Order, select 1 of the Orders below, then select 1 eligible friendly unit within 6" of that <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> model that receives the Order.
- **Move! Move! Move**! - Add 3" to the Movement characteristic of models in this unit.
- **Fix bayonets!** - Improve the Weapon Skill, Initiative and Strength characteristics of models in this unit by 1.
- **Take aim!** - Improve the Ballistic Skill characteristic of models in this unit by 1, and increase the Armour Penetration characteristic of Ranged weapons in this unit by 1.
- **First rank, fire! Second rank, fire!** - Ranged weapons in the unit gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="sustained-hits-1" data-type="keyword">Sustained Hits 1</span> ability. If the weapon already possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="sustained-hits-x" data-type="keyword">Sustained Hits [X]</span> ability, increase [X] by 1. This Order does not affect Ranged weapons with the <span class="keyword" data-term="torrent" data-type="keyword">Torrent</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="blast-x" data-type="keyword">Blast [X]"</span> ability.
- **Take cover!** - Improve the Armour Save characteristic (to a maximum of 2+) of models in this unit by 1.
- **Duty and honour!** - Increase the Leadership characteristic of this unit to 5+ and reduce the cost of the <span class="keyword" data-term="mission-task" data-type="action">Mission Task</span> Action to 1 Activation Point.

The unit you selected is affected by that Order until the end of your next Command Phase. Unless otherwise stated, a unit can only be affected by 1 Order at a time (any Order subsequently issued to that unit replaces the current one). If a unit being affected by an Order becomes Battle Shocked, that Order ceases to affect that unit.

Certain <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> units' Voice of Command section may also offer more specific approaches to issuing Orders, or may simply state that they can issue Orders to any Astra Militarum unit.


##### Regimental Coordination


Each time a Command Squad in your army is Activated, <span class="keyword" data-term="regiment" data-type="keyword">Regiment</span> units that received an Order from a model leading that Command Squad can also be Activated. Every <span class="keyword" data-term="regiment" data-type="keyword">Regiment</span> unit activated in this manner must all complete the same Action at the same time, or skip the Action entirely. 

***Example:**** You Activate a Command Squad that issued the 'Take Aim!' Order to 1 Infantry Section and 1 Heavy Weapons Section in your Command Phase. All 3 of these units are now a part of this Activation. You then decide to spend your first Activation with the *<span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span>* Action. However, you elect to keep the Command Squad and the Heavy Weapons Section in their current positions, but move the Infantry Section. All 3 of those units count as having spent their first Activation Point on that *<span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span>*. You then proceed to spend your second Activation Point on a *<span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span>* Action, choosing to make Ranged attacks with all 3 of the units. Correspondingly, all 3 of those units count as having spent their second Activation Point on that *<span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span>* Action. You then proceed to spend your third Activation Point on a *<span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span>* Action, but only the Infantry Section can complete this action as they were the only unit that actually moved as part of the *<span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span>* Action (which is a requirement of the *<span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span>* Action). Regardless, all 3 units count as having spent their third Activation Point on that *<span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span>* Action. *


## Detachment Traits

Your Astra Militarum army possesses 2 Detachment Points it can use to select any of the following Detachment Traits. 


<h6 id="combined-arms-task-force">Combined Arms Task Force<span class="heading-value">2 Detachment Points</span></h6>


Increase the number of Orders that <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> units are eligible to issue by 1. <span class="keyword" data-term="leaders" data-type="keyword">Leaders</span> that can issue Orders to <span class="keyword" data-term="regiment" data-type="keyword">Regiment</span> units can also issue Orders to <span class="keyword" data-term="squadron" data-type="keyword">Squadron</span> and <span class="keyword" data-term="artillery" data-type="keyword">Artillery</span> units.


<h6 id="planetary-defense-force">Planetary Defense Force<span class="heading-value">2 Detachment Points</span></h6>


Each time an Astra Militarum unit <span class="keyword" data-term="remains-stationary" data-type="action">Remains Stationary</span>, until the end of the turn, Ranged attacks by models in that unit have the <span class="keyword" data-term="lethal-hits" data-type="keyword">Lethal Hits</span> ability. 

If at least 1 model in an Astra Militarum unit is positioned on an Objective Marker, increase the Ballistic Skill characteristic of models in that unit by 1.


<h6 id="tempestus-scions-task-force">Tempestus Scions Task Force<span class="heading-value">2 Detachment Points</span></h6>


Your army can only include units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="militarum-tempestus" data-type="keyword">Militarum Tempestus</span> keyword or the <span class="keyword" data-term="militarum-auxilia" data-type="keyword">Militarum Auxilia</span> keyword.

Militarum Tempestus Scions gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword.

The Infantry Section unit loses the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword.

Militarum Tempestus Scions units can utilize the Regimental Coordination Army Rule when under the effects of an Order issued by a Tempestor Prime leading a Militarum Tempestus Scions Command Squad.

The Valkyrie unit: 
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="militarum-tempestus" data-type="keyword">Militarum Tempestus</span> keyword
- Increases its Ballistic Skill to 5
- Increases its Points cost by 20 points.


<h6 id="armoured-spearhead">Armoured Spearhead<span class="heading-value">1 Detachment Point</span></h6>
