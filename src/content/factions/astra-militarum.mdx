## Army Rules

##### Voice of Command {#voice-of-command}

If your Army Faction is Astra Militarum, Leader models from your army with this ability can issue Orders. Each Leader’s datasheet will specify how many Orders it can issue and which units are eligible to receive those Orders as part of its ‘Voice of Command’ ability. 
Leader units can issue Orders under one of the following scenarios:
- When the Leader is on the battlefield or embarked in a Transport
- Immediately after the Leader Enters the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves.
Each time an Leader model issues an Order, select one of the Orders below, then select 1 eligible friendly unit within 6" of that Leader model to issue it to.
**Move! Move! Move**! - Add 3" to the Move characteristic of models in this unit.
**Fix bayonets!** - Improve the Weapon Skill, Initiative and Strength characteristics of models in this unit by 1.
**Take aim!** - Improve the Ballistic Skill characteristic of models in this unit by 1, and increase the Armour Penetration characteristic of Ranged weapons in this unit by 1.
**First rank, fire! Second rank, fire!** – Ranged weapons in the unit gain the Sustained Hits 1 ability. If the weapon already possesses the Sustained Hits [X] ability, increase [X] by 1. This Order does not affect Ranged weapons with the Torrent or Blast [X]” ability.
**Take cover!** - Improve the Armour Save characteristic of models in this unit by 1 (this cannot improve a model’s Save to better than 2+).
**Duty and honour!** - Increase the Leadership characteristic of this unit to 5+ and reduce the cost of the Mission Task Action to 1 Activation Point.

The unit you selected is affected by that Order until the end of your next Command Phase. Unless otherwise stated, a unit can only be affected by one Order at a time (any Order subsequently issued to that unit replaces the current one). If a unit being affected by an Order becomes Battle Shocked, that Order ceases to affect that unit.

##### Regimental Coordination {#regimental-coordination}

Each time a Command Squad in your army is Activated, Regiment units that received an Order from a model leading that Command Squad can also be Activated. Every Regiment unit activated in this manner must all complete the same Action at the same time, or skip the Action entirely. 
***Example: ****Y**ou Activate a Command Squad that issued the ‘Take Aim**!**’ Order to 1 Infantry Section and 1 Heavy Weapons Section in your Command Phase. All 3 of these units are now a part of this Activation. You then decide **to **spend your first **Activation with the *Move* Action. However, you elect to keep the Command Squad and the Heavy Weapons Section in their current positions, but move the Infantry Section. All 3 of those units count as having spent their first Activation Point on that *Move*. You then proceed to spend your second Activation Point on a *Shoot* Action, choosing to make Ranged attacks with all 3 of the units. Correspondingly, all 3 of those units count as having spent their second Activation Point on that *Shoot* Action. You then proceed to spend your third Activation Point on a *Charge and Fight* Action, but only the Infantry Section can complete this action as they were the only unit that actually moved as part of the *Move* Action (which is a requirement of the *Charge and Fight* Action). Regardless, all 3 units count as having spent their third Activation Point on that *Charge and Fight* Action. *


## Detachment Traits

Your Astra Militarum army possess 2 Detachment Points it can use to select any of the following Detachment Traits. Some of these detachment traits offer abilities that enhance aspects of how your army will be structured and function on the tabletop. 

###### Combined Arms Task Force	2 Detachment Points {#combined-arms-task-force-2-detachment-points}

- Increase the number of Orders that Leader units are eligible to issue by 1. 
- Leaders that can issue Orders to Regiment units can also issue Orders to Squadron units.

###### Planetary Defense Force	2 Detachment Points {#planetary-defense-force-2-detachment-points}

- Each time an Astra Militarum unit Remains Stationary, until the end of the turn, Ranged attacks by models in that unit have the Lethal Hits ability. 
- If at least 1 model in an Astra Militarum unit is positioned on an Objective Marker, increase the Ballistic Skill characteristic of models in that unit by 1.

###### Tempestus Scions Task Force	2 Detachment Points {#tempestus-scions-task-force-2-detachment-points}

- Militarum Tempestus Scions gain the Battleline keyword.
- Militarum Tempestus Scions units can utilize the Regimental Coordination Army Rule when under the effects of an Order issued by a Tempestor Prime leading a Militarum Tempestus Scions Command Squad.
- The Valkyrie unit: 
- Gains the Militarum Tempestus keyword
- Increases its Ballistic Skill to 5
- Increases its Points cost by 20 points.
- The Infantry Section unit loses the Battleline keyword.

###### Armoured Spearhead	1 Detachment Point {#armoured-spearhead-1-detachment-point}

- The Leman Russ Battle Tank gains the Battleline keyword. 
- The Infantry Section unit loses the Battleline keyword.
- Units with the Squadron keyword can use the Shoot Action in the same Activation in which they use the Mission Task Action. However, they can only use 1 Ranged Weapon for that Shoot Action.

###### Cavalry Brigade	1 Detachment Point {#cavalry-brigade-1-detachment-point}

- The Rough Riders unit gains the Battleline keyword.
- The Infantry Section unit loses the Battleline keyword.

###### Expert Bombardiers	1 Detachment Point {#expert-bombardiers-1-detachment-point}

Artillery models making ranged attacks using weapons with the Indirect Fire ability against enemy units that are not in line of sight gain the Sustained Hits 1 ability (or improve an existing Sustained Hits [X] ability by 1) for those attacks. Change this ability to Sustained Hits 2 (or improve an existing Sustained Hits [X] ability by 2) when attacks with those weapons are made against enemy units that are in direct line of sight of the attacking unit. 

###### Mechanized Formations	1 Detachment Point {#mechanized-formations-1-detachment-point}

When an Astra Militarum Regiment unit and a Transport unit are wholly within 6” of one another, each unit improves the Armour Piercing characteristic of Ranged attacks by 1. 

###### Death World Devils	1 Detachment Point {#death-world-devils-1-detachment-point}

Astra Militarum Infantry Regiment units improve their Strength and Initiative characteristics by 1 and gain the Stealth ability when wholly positioned within a Terrain Feature with the Forest keyword.
This detachment cannot be taken in conjunction with the following other detachment traits: City Fighters, Border World Ghosts. 

###### Border World Ghosts	1 Detachment Point {#border-world-ghosts-1-detachment-point}

Astra Militarum Infantry Regiment units gain the Stealth and Infiltrators* *keywords.
This detachment cannot be taken in conjunction with the following other detachment traits: City Fighters, Death World Devils. 

###### Swift as the Wind	1 Detachment Point {#swift-as-the-wind-1-detachment-point}

Astra Militarum Regiment* *and Squadron units improve their Movement characteristic by 1” and their Initiative characteristic by 1.

###### Hardened Chem-Worlders	1 Detachment Point {#hardened-chem-worlders-1-detachment-point}

Astra Militarum Regiment* *Infantry units gain the Feel No Pain 6+ ability.

###### Grim Determination	1 Detachment Point {#grim-determination-1-detachment-point}

- Astra Militarum Infantry units gain the Fearless keywords
- When an Infantry unit is positioned within range of a Primary Objective Marker it gains Light Cover if it does not already gain it. If it is already benefitting from Light Cover or Heavy Cover (due to terrain traits, for example), change the Armour Save characteristic of models in that unit to 4+.
- Add 3” to Charge rolls made for Infantry units that are using the Charge and Fight Action against enemy unit with at least 1 model positioned within range of a Primary Objective Marker.

###### City Fighters	1 Detachment Point {#city-fighters-1-detachment-point}

Astra Militarum Regiment* *Infantry models wholly within 1” of a terrain feature with the Building or Fortification keywords increase their Movement characteristic by 2”, and change their Armour Save characteristic to 4+ when receiving the benefit of Heavy Cover.
This detachment cannot be taken in conjunction with the following other detachment traits: Border World Ghosts, Death World Devils.


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Vox Caster	10 Points {#vox-caster-10-points}
