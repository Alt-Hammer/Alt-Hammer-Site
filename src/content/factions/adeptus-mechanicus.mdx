## Army Rules

##### Doctrina Imperatives {#doctrina-imperatives}

At the start of the battle round, select 1 of the following Doctrina Imperatives to be active for each Techpriest Leader in your army that is currently on the battlefield or embarked in a Transport. That Doctrina Imperative can then be active for any Adeptus Mechanicus Skitarii units with at least 1 model within 6” of that Techpriest Leader:

###### Conqueror Imperative {#conqueror-imperative}

- Increase Weapon Skill characteristic by 1
- Increase Movement characteristic by 2”
- Increase Initiative characteristic by 1

###### Defender Imperative {#defender-imperative}

- Increase Ballistic Skill characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 2
- Gain the Feel No Pain 5+ ability


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Biomechanical Masterworks	50 Points {#biomechanical-masterworks-50-points}

This upgrade can only be taken by your Warlord. This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic by 2”
- Increases its Weapons Skill by 2
- Increases its Ballistics Skill by 2
- Increases its Initiative characteristic by 2
- Increases its Strength characteristic by 2
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increases its Wounds characteristic by 3
- Gains the Feel No Pain 5+ ability
- Must have a base greater than 60mm

###### Microvoid Shield Generator	10 Points {#microvoid-shield-generator-10-points}

The bearer gains the Invulnerable Save 5+ ability against Ranged attacks.

###### Servitor Auto-Proxy {#servitor-auto-proxy}

- Allows Servitor models in a unit being led by a Techpriest to benefit from the Doctrina Imperative activated by that Techpriest for the Battle Round.
- Expands Doctrina Imperative range for Skitarii units from 6” to 12”

###### Techno-Kit {#techno-kit}

- During the Command Phase, Return Up to D3 models that were destroyed during the last Battle Round 
- Each model must first pass a Battle Shock Test before it can be returned to the battlefield. If it fails, the model is permanently destroyed for the remainder of the battle.

###### Breacher Plate {#breacher-plate}

- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Increase Wounds characteristic by 2

###### Enhanced Data-Tether {#enhanced-data-tether}

Allows Skitarii Infantry units to be treated as in range for a Doctrina Imperative when they are within 6” of another Skitarii Infantry unit that is within Doctrina Imperative range, even if no models in the bearer’s unit are within 6” of a Techpriest Leader.

###### Broad Spectrum Data-Tether {#broad-spectrum-data-tether}

Allows Skitarii Vehicle models to be treated as in range for a Doctrina Imperative when they are within 6” of another Skitarii unit that is within Doctrina Imperative range, even if no models in the bearer’s unit is within 6” of a Techpriest Leader.

###### Twin-Linked	Varies (see below)	 {#twin-linked-varies-see-below}

Weapon gains the Twin-linked ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 
Twin-linked weapons have 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.

###### Smoke Launchers	10 Points {#smoke-launchers-10-points}

The bearer can make 2 attacks with Smoke Grenades as part of a Shoot action.

1 Skitarii model can be equipped with an Omnispex.
1 Skitarii model can be equipped with an Enhanced Data-tether.
