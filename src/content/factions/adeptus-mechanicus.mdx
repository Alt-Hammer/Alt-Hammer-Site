## Army Rules

##### Doctrina Imperatives


At the start of the battle round, select 1 of the following Doctrina Imperatives to be active for each <span class="keyword" data-term="techpriest" data-type="keyword">Techpriest</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> in your army that is currently on the battlefield or embarked in a <span class="keyword" data-term="transport" data-type="keyword">Transport</span>. That Doctrina Imperative can then be active for any <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-mechanicus" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Mechanicus</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="skitarii" data-type="keyword">Skitarii</span> units with at least 1 model within 6” of that <span class="keyword" data-term="techpriest" data-type="keyword">Techpriest</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span>:


###### Conqueror Imperative

- Increase Weapon Skill characteristic by 1
- Increase Movement characteristic by 2”
- Increase Initiative characteristic by 1

###### Defender Imperative

- Increase Ballistic Skill characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 2
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span> ability


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Biomechanical Masterworks	50 Points


This upgrade can only be taken by your Warlord. This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic by 2”
- Increases its Weapons Skill by 2
- Increases its Ballistics Skill by 2
- Increases its Initiative characteristic by 2
- Increases its Strength characteristic by 2
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increases its Wounds characteristic by 3
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span> ability
- Must have a base greater than 60mm

###### Microvoid Shield Generator	10 Points


The bearer gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability against Ranged attacks.


###### Servitor Auto-Proxy	8 Points

- Allows Servitor models in a unit being led by a <span class="keyword" data-term="techpriest" data-type="keyword">Techpriest</span> to benefit from the Doctrina Imperative activated by that Techpriest for the Battle Round.
- Expands Doctrina Imperative range for <span class="keyword" data-term="skitarii" data-type="keyword">Skitarii</span> units from 6” to 12”

###### Techno-Kit	10 Points

- During the Command Phase, Return Up to D3 models that were destroyed during the last Battle Round 
- Each model must first pass a Battle Shock Test before it can be returned to the battlefield. If it fails, the model is permanently destroyed for the remainder of the battle.

###### Breacher Plate	12 Points

- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Increase Wounds characteristic by 2

###### Enhanced Data-Tether	8 Points


Allows <span class="keyword" data-term="skitarii" data-type="keyword">Skitarii</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> units to be treated as in range for a Doctrina Imperative when they are within 6” of another <span class="keyword" data-term="skitarii" data-type="keyword">Skitarii</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> unit that is within Doctrina Imperative range, even if no models in the bearer’s unit are within 6” of a <span class="keyword" data-term="techpriest-leader" data-type="keyword">Techpriest Leader</span>.


###### Broad Spectrum Data-Tether	8 Points


Allows <span class="keyword" data-term="skitarii-vehicle" data-type="keyword">Skitarii Vehicle</span> models to be treated as in range for a Doctrina Imperative when they are within 6” of another <span class="keyword" data-term="skitarii" data-type="keyword">Skitarii</span> unit that is within Doctrina Imperative range, even if no models in the bearer’s unit are within 6” of a <span class="keyword" data-term="techpriest-leader" data-type="keyword">Techpriest Leader</span>.


###### Twin-Linked	Varies (see below)


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet.

Twin-linked weapons have 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.

###### Smoke Launchers	10 Points


The bearer can make 2 attacks with <span class="weapon-ref" data-weapon="smoke-grenades">Smoke Grenades</span> as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span> action.
