## Army Rules

##### Acts of Faith


At the start of each Command Phase, roll 1 D6. The resulting roll generates a number of Miracle Dice. Miracle Dice are primarily used by your <span class="keyword" data-term="adepta-sororitas" data-type="keyword">Adepta Sororitas</span> units to perform Acts of Faith. An Act of Faith involves using Miracle Dice for several kinds of dice rolls, replacing what the results of that dice roll would be with an ability conferred by the Miracle Dice used for it instead. 

Eligible rolls that can be substituted with a Miracle Dice as an Act of Faith, and the effect that Miracle Dice confers, is described below:
- **Miracle Dice for a Hit Roll**: The Hit counts as a Critical Hit
- **Miracle Dice for a Wound Roll:** The Wound counts as a Critical Wound.
- **Miracle Dice for an Armour Save: **The** **Armour Save is successful.
- **Miracle Dice for Battle Shock Tests (requires 2 Miracle Dice to use):** The Battle Shock test is passed, and the unit cannot become Battle Shocked for the remainder of the Battle Round.

Unless otherwise stated:
- Each Act of Faith performed by an <span class="keyword" data-term="adepta-sororitas" data-type="keyword">Adepta Sororitas</span> unit can use a maximum of 1 Miracle Dice
- Units can use 1 Act of Faith per Battle Round.

It should be noted that the face values of Miracle Dice are never relevant and do not impact their abilities described above; the Dice is simply used as a marker. Players are encouraged to have a different set of dice to use as their Miracle Dice pool to ensure Miracle Dice used can be clearly identified.


##### Holy Trinity


Each time an <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-ministorum" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Ministorum</span> unit from your army makes a Ranged attack at an enemy unit with at least 1 Bolt weapon, 1 <span class="keyword" data-term="torrent" data-type="keyword">Torrent</span> weapon and 1 <span class="keyword" data-term="melta-x" data-type="keyword">Melta [X]</span> weapon, add 1 to the Wound rolls for those attacks. 

“Bolt weapons” include the following: 
- Bolt Pistol
- Boltgun
- Storm Bolter
- Condemnor Boltgun
- Heavy Bolter


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Simulacrum Imperialis	8 points


While the bearer is within range of a Primary Objective Marker, the bearer and friendly models within 12” of them increase their Objective Control value by 1.


###### Cherubin	5 Points per Model


Units eligible to be equipped with Cherubin will state how many the unit can be equipped with. Each time a unit uses the <span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span> Action, you can declare that the unit is using a Cherubin as part of that Action. When you do so, select 1 model in the unit making a Ranged attack. That model increases the Attacks characteristic of the Ranged weapon they are using by 2. One those attacks are completed, remove the Cherubin model from the battlefield. A unit can use any number of Cherubin you choose as part of a Shoot Action, but models in the unit can only benefit from 1 Cherubin ability at a time. In other words, 2 Cherubin cannot be used on 1 model to increase that model’s Ranged attacks characteristic by 4.
