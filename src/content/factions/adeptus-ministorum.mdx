## Army Rules

##### Acts of Faith {#acts-of-faith}

At the start of each Command Phase, roll 1 D6. The resulting roll generates a number of Miracle Dice. Miracle Dice are used by your Adepta Sororitas units to perform Acts of Faith. An Act of Faith involves using Miracle Dice for several kinds of dice rolls, replacing what the results of that dice roll would be with an ability conferred by the Miracle Dice used for it instead. Eligible rolls that can be substituted with a Miracle Dice as an Act of Faith, and the effect that Miracle Dice confers, is described below:
**Miracle Dice for a Hit Roll**: The Hit counts as a Critical Hit
**Miracle Dice for a Wound Roll:** The Wound counts as a Critical Wound.
**Miracle Dice for an Armour Save: **The** **Armour Save is successful.
**Miracle Dice for Battle**** S****hock Tests (requires 2 Miracle Dice to use):** The Battle Shock test is passed, and the unit cannot become Battle Shocked for the remainder of the Battle Round.
It should be noted that the face values of Miracle Dice are never relevant and do not impact their abilities described above; the Dice is simply used as a marker. Players are encouraged to have a different set of dice to use as their Miracle Dice pool to ensure Miracle Dice used can be clearly identified.

##### Holy Trinity {#holy-trinity}

Each time an Adeptus Ministorum unit from your army makes a Ranged attack at an enemy unit with at least 1 Bolt weapon, 1 Torrent weapon and 1 Melta [X] weapon, add 1 to the Wound rolls for those attacks. “Bolt weapons” include the following: Bolt Pistol, Boltgun, Storm Bolter, Condemnor Boltgun, Heavy Bolter.


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Simulacrum Imperialis	8 points {#simulacrum-imperialis-8-points}

While the bearer is within range of a Primary Objective Marker, the bearer and friendly models within 12” of them increase their Objective Control value by 1.

###### Cherubin	5 Points per Model {#cherubin-5-points-per-model}

Units eligible to be equipped with Cherubin will state how many the unit can be equipped with. Each time a unit uses the Shoot Action, you can declare that the unit is using a Cherubin as part of that Action. When you do so, select 1 model in the unit making a Ranged attack. That model increases the Attacks characteristic of the Ranged weapon they are using by 2. One those attacks are completed, remove the Cherubin model from the battlefield. A unit can use any number of Cherubin you choose as part of a Shoot Action, but models in the unit can only benefit from 1 Cherubin ability at a time. In other words, 2 Cherubin cannot be used on 1 model to increase that model’s Ranged attacks characteristic by 4.

###### Relic Weapon	10 points per weapon {#relic-weapon-10-points-per-weapon}

- Increase weapon’s Attacks characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Damage characteristic by 1

###### Combi-Bolter	15 points {#combi-bolter-15-points}

Models equipped with a Boltgun can upgrade that Boltgun to a Combi-bolter that also includes 1 of the following Ranged weapon options:
- Flamer
- Meltagun
- Plasma Gun

###### Rod of Office	10 Points {#rod-of-office-10-points}

- Grants precision to the unit’s weapons.

###### Artificer Armour	10 Points {#artificer-armour-10-points}

- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Add the *Feel No Pain: 5+ *ability.

###### Iron Halo	25 Points {#iron-halo-25-points}

- The bearer is considered to have a Toughness characteristic of 6 against Ranged attacks.
- Gain the Invulnerable Save 4+ ability

###### Iron Surplice	10 Points {#iron-surplice-10-points}

- Add 1 to Toughness characteristic
- Add 2 to Wounds characteristic
- Cannot be taken by a model equipped with a Jump Pack

###### Storm Shield	15 points {#storm-shield-15-points}

- Increase Wounds characteristic by 2
- Cannot be equipped by a model with the *Mounted* keyword

###### Jump Pack	10 Points {#jump-pack-10-points}

- Increase Movement characteristic to 12”
- Add the *Deep Strike *ability
- Add the *Jump Pack* keyword.
- Models equipped with a Jump Pack cannot be deployed in or embark in a Transport
- Models equipped with a Jump Pack cannot be equipped with Ranged weapons that have the Heavy keyword.

###### Paragon Warsuit	40 Points {#paragon-warsuit-40-points}

- Gain the Walker keyword
- Increase Movement characteristic to 8”
- Increase Strength characteristic to 5
- Increase Toughness characteristic to 5
- Increase Wounds characteristic by 3
- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+

Models equipped with a Paragon Warsuit replace any weapon upgrades they may take and instead select from the following:
- Must be equipped with 1 of the following Ranged weapons:
- Heavy Bolter
- Heavy Flamer
- Multi-melta
- Must be equipped with 1 of the following Melee weapons:
- Paragon War Blade
- Paragon War Mace
- Can be equipped with 1 of the following Pistols:
- Bolt Pistol
- Hand Flamer
- Inferno Pistol
- Can be equipped with up to 2x Frag Grenades and 2x Krak Grenades

Models equipped with a Paragon Warsuit can only lead units that are also equipped with Paragon Warsuits.

###### Twin-linked Weapon	Varies (see below) {#twin-linked-weapon-varies-see-below}

Weapon gains the Twin-linked ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 
Twin-linked weapons have 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.

###### Dozer Blade	20 Points {#dozer-blade-20-points}

Models equipped with a Dozer Blade improve their Breach Test roll by 1 and do not reduce their Movement characteristic by 2” when moving through a terrain feature.
Additionally, models equipped with a Dozer Blade modify their Melee attack as follows:
- Increase Attacks characteristic by 3
- Increase Strength characteristic by 2
- Increase Armour Piercing characteristic by 1 (ie, AP 0 attacks become AP -1)
- Increase Damage characteristic by 1

###### Smoke Launchers	10 Points {#smoke-launchers-10-points}

The bearer can make 2 attacks with Smoke Grenades as part of a Shoot action.

This model can be equipped with any of the following:
- Artificer Armour
- Iron Halo
- Iron Surplice
- Rod of Office

This model can be equipped with a Jump Pack. Refer to the Jump Pack upgrade under Wargear Upgrades for ability details and limitations with respect to weapons and wargear upgrades.
This model can be equipped with a Paragon Warsuit. If it does so, Weapon Options and Wargear Options detailed in this unit’s profile are replaced with those detailed under the Paragon Warsuit upgrade’s options.
Any weapon equipped by this model (excluding grenades) can be upgraded with the Relic Weapon upgrade.
