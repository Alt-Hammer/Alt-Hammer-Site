## Army Rules

##### Pursuit of Perfection


Units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="slaanesh" data-type="keyword">Slaanesh</span> keyword gain 1 of the following abilities each time they destroy an enemy unit:
- With a Ranged attack:
- Score Critical Wounds for Ranged attacks on a 4+
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="precision" data-type="keyword">Precision</span> keyword to Ranged weapons equipped by the unit
- Reduce Leadership characteristic of enemy units by 2 when taking Battle Shock test after suffering casualties inflicted by Ranged weapons in this unit.

- With a Melee attack:
- Score Critical Hits for Melee attacks on a 4+
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="precision" data-type="keyword">Precision</span> keyword to Melee weapons equipped by the unit
- Reduce Leadership characteristic of enemy units with at least 1 model within 3” of this unit.

Each new ability gained remains in effect until the end of the Battle.


##### Blessings of Slaanesh


When a friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="slaanesh-infantry" data-type="keyword">Slaanesh Infantry</span> unit has a starting strength of 6 models:
- Increase the Leadership characteristic of models in the unit by 1
- During your first Command Phase, select up to 2 **Pursuit of Perfection** abilities to be in effect at the start of the battle.
- Add 1 to the Objective Control value of models in that unit

##### Daemonhost


Up to 50% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the Chaos Daemons Faction, but these units must possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="slaanesh" data-type="keyword">Slaanesh</span> keyword.
