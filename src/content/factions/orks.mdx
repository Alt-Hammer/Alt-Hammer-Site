## Army Rules

##### Waaaaagh!

While your Warlord is on the battlefield (but not embarked in a <span class="keyword" data-term="transport" data-type="keyword">Transport</span>), each <span class="keyword" data-term="ork" data-type="keyword">Ork</span> unit that can draw line of sight to your Warlord gain abilities based on the actions of your Warlord, as follows:
- If your Warlord made a Ranged attack this Battle Round, <span class="keyword" data-term="ork" data-type="keyword">Ork</span> units that have not yet been activated improve the Ballistic Skill characteristic of models in the unit by 1.
- If your Warlord successfully Charged an enemy unit this Battle Round, <span class="keyword" data-term="ork" data-type="keyword">Ork</span> units that have not yet been activated increase their Weapons Skill characteristic by 1.
- If your Warlord made a Melee attack this Battle Round, <span class="keyword" data-term="ork" data-type="keyword">Ork</span> units that have not yet been activated score Critical Hits for Melee attacks on a 5+ instead of a 6+.

If your Warlord is destroyed, all of the above abilities become permanently activated for all <span class="keyword" data-term="ork" data-type="keyword">Ork</span> units in your army for the remainder of the battle.

##### Nob Taktiks

Each <span class="keyword" data-term="ork" data-type="keyword">Ork</span> unit that includes a Nob can use this ability while that unit (and its Nob) are on the battlefield, including while embarked in a <span class="keyword" data-term="transport" data-type="keyword">Transport</span>. To do so, when the unit is Activated, roll a D6, subtracting 1 if the unit is currently Battle Shocked. On a 4+, select 1 of the following Nob Taktiks to be in effect for the unit until the end of the Battle Round:

###### More Dakka!

- The unit scores Critical Hits on a 5+ when making Ranged attacks at a single enemy unit.
- Increase the Armour Piercing characteristic of Ranged weapons in the unit by 1 if the entire unit targets a single enemy unit.
- Reduce the Movement characteristic of models in the unit by 1”
- Reduce the Initiative characteristic of models in the unit by 1

###### Krump ‘Em!

- Increase the Movement characteristic of models in the unit by 1”
- The unit scores Critical Hits on a 5+ when making Melee attacks.
- Melee weapons equipped by the unit gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="sustained-hits-1" data-type="keyword">Sustained Hits 1</span> ability, or increase [X] by 1 if it already possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="sustained-hits-x" data-type="keyword">Sustained Hits [X]</span> ability.

###### Straightn’ Out!

This Taktik can only be selected by a unit that is currently Battle Shocked. When selected, destroy 1 model from the unit. Once the model is removed, the unit Rallies and increases its Leadership characteristic by 1 until the end of your next Command Phase.

##### The Beast Snaggas Clan

When a <span class="keyword" data-term="beast-snaggas" data-type="keyword">Beast Snaggas</span> unit makes an attack against an enemy unit with the <span class="keyword" data-term="beast" data-type="keyword">Beast</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="monster" data-type="keyword">Monster</span> keyword, increase the Strength characteristic of that attack by 1. If the attack scores a Critical Wound, increase the Damage characteristic of that attack by 1 as well.


## Detachment Traits

Your Ork army can select 1 of the following Detachment Traits. These detachment traits offer force composition and army structure changes to represent various infamous Ork formations and their warfare styles on the tabletop. 

###### Green Tide

Each time a <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> unit from your army is destroyed, roll a D6. On a roll of a 4 or 5, up to half of the models in the unit can be placed into Strategic Reserves and may be Activated in the next Battle Round. On a roll of a 6, the whole unit can be placed into Strategic Reserves and may be Activated in the next Battle Round.

###### Speed Freeks

- Bikerz gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- Boyz lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- Any <span class="keyword" data-term="orks-infantry" data-type="keyword">Orks Infantry</span> units in your army that do not have the <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="jump-pack" data-type="keyword">Jump Pack</span> keywords must start the battle embarked in a Transport.
- Your army cannot include any <span class="keyword" data-term="beast-snaggas" data-type="keyword">Beast Snaggas </span>units

###### Snaggin’ Raid

- Beast Snaggas Boyz gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- Boyz lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- Only units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="beast-snaggas" data-type="keyword">Beast Snaggas</span> keyword can be included in your army
