## Army Rules

##### And They Shall Know No Fear


<span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes</span> models ignore any and all modifiers to their Leadership characteristics when making Battle Shock tests; Leadership values are never modified for any reason, including enemy unit abilities that would otherwise have such an effect. Further, units automatically Rally at the start of the next Command Phase after being Battle Shocked in the prior Battle Round.


##### Angels of Death


If your army’s Warlord possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="imperium" data-type="keyword">Imperium</span> keyword, but does not possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes</span> keyword - up to 25% of the total points cost of your army can include units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes</span> keyword.


## Detachment Traits

Adeptus Astartes detachments are made up of Detachment Traits that reflect the combat doctrine preferences of their Chapter. Your Adeptus Astartes detachment has 2 Detachment Trait Points that you may allocate when constructing your army list by selecting Detachment Traits from the following options. 


#### Chapter Identity Detachment Traits


Several Detachment Traits reflect unique identities found among legendary Adeptus Astartes Chapters such as the Ultramarines, Blood Angels, Imperial Fists, and their successor chapters. Your army may select up to 1 of these Detachment Traits.


###### Adepts of the Codex Astartes	1 Detachment Point


At the start of your Command phase, you can select one of the Combat Doctrines listed below. Until the start of your next Command phase, that Combat Doctrine is active and its effects apply to all <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> units from your army. You can only select each Combat Doctrine once per battle, and the effects of an active Combat Doctrine are no longer usable after you select a new Combat Doctrine to be active.

- **Devastator Doctrine:** units increase their Ballistic Skill by 1 when they <span class="keyword" data-term="remain-stationary" data-type="action">Remain Stationary</span>.
- **Tactical Doctrine:** units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="unyielding" data-type="keyword">Unyielding</span> ability.
- **Assault Doctrine:** units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> keyword. 

###### Souls of Iron	1 Detachment Point

- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes-infantry" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes Infantry</span> models ignore the effects of the <span class="keyword" data-term="heavy" data-type="keyword">Heavy</span> keyword when making attacks.
- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes </span>units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="lethal-hits" data-type="keyword">Lethal Hits</span> ability when making attacks against enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="vehicle" data-type="keyword">Vehicle</span> units.
- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes </span>units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span> ability against Ranged attacks.

###### Born in the Saddle	1 Detachment Point

- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes-mounted" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes Mounted</span> units gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> and <span class="keyword" data-term="unyielding" data-type="keyword">Unyielding</span> keywords.
- Melee attacks by <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="lance" data-type="keyword">Lance</span> ability as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span> action.
- All <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> units must start the Battle embarked in a Transport unless they possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability, in which case they can start the battle in Strategic Reserves as normal.

###### Blood Lust	1 Detachment Point

- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes-infantry" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes Infantry</span> units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> ability.
- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes-infantry" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes Infantry</span> units add 1” to the distance they can move when they <span class="keyword" data-term="advance" data-type="action">Advance</span> as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span>* *action, and when they use the <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span> action.
- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes-infantry" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes Infantry</span> units must always move towards the closest enemy unit when they <span class="keyword" data-term="advance" data-type="action">Advance</span> as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span>* *action, and must always select the closest enemy unit as the target of a <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span> action.
- <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes</span> units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="devastating-wounds" data-type="keyword">Devastating Wounds</span> ability when making melee attacks as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span> action. If a melee weapon being used already has the <span class="keyword" data-term="devastating-wounds" data-type="keyword">Devastating Wounds</span> ability in these instances, critical wounds from attacks with that weapon are scored on a 5+ instead of a 6+, or have no additional effect if the weapon already possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="anti-keyword-x" data-type="keyword">Anti-[KEYWORD] [X+]</span> ability.

###### Implacable Defenders	1 Detachment Point

Units that already have the <span class="keyword" data-term="stealth" data-type="keyword">Stealth</span> ability instead increase their Movement characteristic by 2”.

Additionally, <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes-infantry" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes Infantry</span> units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="stealth" data-type="keyword">Stealth</span> ability gain access to the following <span class="keyword" data-term="return-to-the-shadows" data-type="action">Return to the Shadows</span> Action.


###### Brotherhood of Inferno	1 Detachment Point

- Add 3 to the Attacks characteristic of <span class="keyword" data-term="torrent" data-type="keyword">Torrent</span> weapons when making ranged attacks against enemy units that do not have the <span class="keyword" data-term="vehicle" data-type="keyword">Vehicle</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="monster" data-type="keyword">Monster</span> keyword.

## Wargear Upgrades

##### Astartes Armour Upgrades


Many Adeptus Astartes units may be upgraded to wear different armour patterns designed to fulfill unique battlefield roles. Armour types and equipment available to an Astartes unit each have their own unique combinations of benefits and limitations that are reflected in how that item modifies a model’s datasheet characteristics, what weapons it can be equipped with, etc. Units that are eligible to upgrade their armour type must do so using the per-model points costs detailed here. Every model in a unit must be equipped with the same armour type, and players should be aware that not all weapon options are available for every armour type. This is identified by weapon in the Adeptus Astartes Weapons Data Table.


###### Tacticus Armour	0 points per Model


With the exclusion of Scouts, all <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes-infantry" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes Infantry</span> models are equipped with Tacticus Armour by default and possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="tacticus" data-type="keyword">Tacticus</span> keyword.


###### Phobos Armour	5 Points per model

- Increase Movement characteristic by 1”
- Increase Initiative Characteristic by 1
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="scout-7" data-type="keyword">Scout 7”</span> ability
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="infiltrator" data-type="keyword">Infiltrator</span> ability

###### Gravis Armour	10 points per model

- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Reduce Initiative Characteristic by 1
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1”
- Ignore the effects of the <span class="keyword" data-term="heavy" data-type="keyword">Heavy</span> keyword when making Attacks
- Models equipped with Gravis armour must be on a 40mm base
- Models equipped with Gravis armour count as 2 models for the purposes of the <span class="keyword" data-term="transport-x" data-type="keyword">Transport [X]</span> ability

###### Terminator Armour	20 Points per model

- Increase Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1”
- Increase Wounds characteristic by 1
- Reduce Initiative Characteristic by 1
- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Ignore the effects of the <span class="keyword" data-term="heavy" data-type="keyword">Heavy</span> keyword when making Attacks
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span>* *ability
- Models must be on a 40mm to 60mm base
- Models count as 2 models for the purposes of the <span class="keyword" data-term="transport-x" data-type="keyword">Transport [X]</span> ability

###### Saturnine Terminator Armour	45 Points per model

- Increase Strength characteristic by 2
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 3
- Increase Wounds characteristic by 3
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1”
- Reduce Initiative Characteristic by 2
- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Ignore the effects of the Heavy keyword when making Attacks
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span>* *ability
- Models equipped with Saturnine Terminator armour must be on a 50mm to 60mm base 
- Models equipped with Saturnine Terminator armour count as 4 models for the purposes of the Transport ability

###### Scout Armour	-10 Points per model

- Increase Movement characteristic by 1”
- Reduce Strength characteristic by 1
- Reduce Toughness characteristic by 1
- Reduce Wounds characteristic by 1
- Reduce Armour Save characteristic to 4+
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="scout-7" data-type="keyword">Scout 7”</span> ability
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="infiltrator" data-type="keyword">Infiltrator</span> ability

##### Astartes Equipment Upgrades


Adeptus Astartes have access to a range of Equipment upgrades that can be selected to suit specific battlefield roles. The datasheet impacts and points cost for each such upgrade is detailed here, and unit datasheets will identify upgrades from this list that the unit is eligible to take.


###### Grav Inhibitors	8 Points per model

- Can only be selected by models equipped with Tacticus armour
- Increase Movement characteristic to 12”
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span>* *ability
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="hover" data-type="keyword">Hover</span> ability.
- Models equipped with a Grav Inhibitors cannot be deployed in or embark in a Transport

###### Jump Pack	10 points per model

- Can only be selected by models equipped with Tacticus or Gravis armour
- Increase Movement characteristic to 12”
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span>* *ability
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="jump-pack" data-type="keyword">Jump Pack</span> keyword.
- Models equipped with a Jump Pack cannot be deployed in or embark in a Transport
- Models equipped with a Jump Pack can only be equipped with weapons that include Jump Pack under its “Weapon Availability for Armour and Wargear” information.

###### Bike	20 points per model

- Can only be selected by models equipped with Tacticus armour
- Increase Movement characteristic to 12”
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span>* *keyword
- Add a Twin-Linked Boltgun to the model’s wargear
- Models equipped with a Bike must be on a 90mm x 52mm oval base or similar
- Models equipped with a Bike can only be equipped with weapons that include Bike under its “Weapon Availability for Armour and Wargear” information.

###### Artificer Armour	15 points per model

- Can only be equipped by a model equipped with Tacticus or Gravis Armour
- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span>* *ability.

###### Riever Gear	10 points per model


A unit equipped with Phobos Armour or Scout Armour can be equipped with Riever Gear, which adds the following abilities:

- **Grapnel Launcher:** Each time the bearer complete a <span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span>, <span class="keyword" data-term="fall-back" data-type="action">Fall Back</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span> action, ignore any vertical distance when determining the total distance the bearer can be moved.
- **Reiver Grav-chute:** Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability.
- **Terror Troops (Aura):** While an enemy unit (excluding <span class="keyword" data-term="monsters" data-type="keyword">Monsters</span> and <span class="keyword" data-term="vehicles" data-type="keyword">Vehicles</span>) is within 3" of one or more models with this ability, subtract 1 from the Leadership characteristic of models in that enemy unit.

###### Occulus	3 Points


Model equipped with a Boltgun or Bolt Rifle only. The bearer’s Boltgun or Bolt Rifle gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="precision" data-type="keyword">Precision</span> keyword.


###### Camo Cloak	5 Points per Model


Models equipped with Scout Armour or Phobos Armour can be equipped with a Camo Cloak. Enemy models attacking a model equipped with a Camo Cloak reduce their Ballistic Skill characteristic by 1 whilst the model equipped with a Camo Cloak is positioned within a Terrain Feature.


###### Iron Halo	20 Points


Models equipped with an Iron Halo:

- Increase their Toughness characteristic by 2 against Ranged attacks
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-4" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 4+</span> ability.

###### Rosarius	20 Points


Models equipped with a Rosarius:

- Increase their Toughness characteristic by 2 against Ranged attacks
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-4" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 4+</span> ability.

###### Storm Shield	15 points

- Increase Wounds characteristic by 2
- Cannot be equipped by models with the <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> keyword.
- Cannot be equipped by models equipped with Phobos Armour.

###### Relic Weapon	10 points per weapon

- Increase weapon’s Attacks characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Damage characteristic by 1
- <span class="keyword" data-term="grenade" data-type="keyword">Grenade</span> weapons cannot select this upgrade

###### Combi-Bolter	15 points


A Combi-Bolter is a Boltgun that must include 1 of the following Ranged weapons:

- Flamer
- Meltagun
- Plasma Gun

###### Chapter Banner	60 points

- Friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes</span> units that can draw line of sight to the bearer, and who have at least 1 model within 18” of that model, change their Leadership characteristic to 2+. 
- While the bearer is on the battlefield and positioned wholly within range of a Primary Objective Marker, it is considered to have an Objective Control value of 30.
- Models equipped with Phobos Armour cannot be equipped with a Chapter Banner.

###### Hero’s Heraldry Banner	20 Points


Units equipped with a Hero’s Heraldry Banner increase their Leadership characteristic as well as the Leadership characteristics of any other models in a unit they are Leading to 3+.


###### Auspex Scanner	10 Points


Units equipped with an Auspex Scanner:

- Ignore the effects of the <span class="keyword" data-term="stealth" data-type="keyword">Stealth</span> ability when making Ranged attacks against units with at least 1 model within 18” that possess that ability. 
- Cannot have enemy models placed within 12” of a model in this unit when that enemy unit uses the  <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> Action using the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability.

###### Twin-linked Weapon	Varies (see below)


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 

Twin-linked weapons have 3 points costs, based on the following: 

- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.

###### Dozer Blade	20 Points


Models equipped with a Dozer Blade improve their Breach Test roll by 1 and do not reduce their Movement characteristic by 2” when moving through a terrain feature.

Additionally, models equipped with a Dozer Blade modify their Melee attack as follows:

- Increase Attacks characteristic by 3
- Increase Strength characteristic by 2
- Increase Armour Piercing characteristic by 1 (ie, AP 0 attacks become AP -1)
- Increase Damage characteristic by 1

###### Smoke Launchers	10 Points


The bearer can make 2 attacks with Smoke Grenades as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span> action.


###### Shield Dome	15 Points


Models equipped with a Shield Dome gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span>* *ability.


###### Orbital Comms Array	20 Points


Models equipped with an Orbital Comms Array allow friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="adeptus-astartes" data-type="keyword">Adeptus Astartes</span> units within 6” of this model to ignore the effects of the <span class="keyword" data-term="stealth" data-type="keyword">Stealth</span> ability when making Ranged attacks against units that possess that ability.
