## Army Rules

##### Guardians Eternal {#guardians-eternal}

Adeptus Custodes models ignore any and all modifiers to their Leadership characteristics when making Battle Shock tests. Further, units automatically Rally at the start of the next Command Phase after being Battle Shocked in the prior Battle Round.

##### Martial Ka’tah {#martial-katah}

Each time an Adeptus Custodes Infantry unit from your army with this ability is selected to fight, select one of the Ka’tah Stances below. Until that unit has finished making its attacks, the selected Stance is active for it and it gains the relevant ability:

###### Dacatarai Stance {#dacatarai-stance}

Increase the Attacks characteristic of models in the unit by 3.

###### Rendax Stance {#rendax-stance}

Increase the Strength characteristic of models in the unit by 2.


## Wargear Upgrades

Adeptus Custodes have access to a range of Wargear and Equipment upgrades that can be selected to suit specific battlefield roles. The datasheet impacts and points cost for each such upgrade is detailed here, and unit datasheets will identify upgrades from this list that the unit is eligible to take.

###### Auric Halo	15 points {#auric-halo-15-points}

- Model gains the *Invulnerable Save 4+* ability

###### Relic Weapon	15 points per weapon {#relic-weapon-15-points-per-weapon}

- Increase weapon’s Attacks characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Damage characteristic by 1

###### Preasidium Shield	15 points {#preasidium-shield-15-points}

- Increase Wounds characteristic by 2
- Cannot be equipped by a model with the *Mounted* keyword

###### Vexilla	20 Points {#vexilla-20-points}

While a model equipped with a Vexilla is on the battlefield and positioned wholly within range of an objective marker, add 30 Wounds to the total Wounds count when determining which player controls that objective for the purpose of scoring Primary Objective Victory Points.

###### Allarus Terminator Armour	30 Points {#allarus-terminator-armour-30-points}

- Increase Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1”
- Increase Wounds characteristic by 1
- Reduce Initiative Characteristic by 1
- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Ignore the effects of the Heavy keyword when making Attacks
- Add the *Deep Strike *ability
- Models equipped with Terminator armour count as 2 models for the purposes of the Transport ability

###### Dawneagle Jetbike	50 Points {#dawneagle-jetbike-50-points}

- Increase Movement characteristic to 14”
- Increase Toughness characteristic to 8
- Add the *Mounted *keyword
- Add the *Deep Strike* keyword
- Replace weapons equipped by the model with the following:
- 1 Interceptor Lance
- 1 of the following:
- Hurricane Bolter
- Cyclone Missile Launcher

###### Smoke Launchers	10 Points {#smoke-launchers-10-points}

The bearer can make 2 attacks with Smoke Grenades as part of a Shoot action.

This model can be equipped with 1 of the following:
- Allarus Terminator Armour
- Dawneagle Jetbike

This model can be equipped with any of the following: 
- Praesidium Shield
- Auric Halo

Any weapon equipped by this model can be upgraded to a Relic Weapon.

###### Leader {#leader}

This model can lead the following units: Custodian Guard, Custodian Wardens, Vertus Praetors (when equipped with a Dawneagle Jetbike only).

##### Blade Champion {#blade-champion}

##### Shield Captain {#shield-captain}


###### Unit Composition {#unit-composition}

1 Shield Captain

###### Standard Wargear {#standard-wargear}

This model is equipped with 1 Guardian Spear with Bolter.

###### Special Abilities {#special-abilities}

**Inspirational Exemplar: **Models in a unit being led by this model score Critical Hits on a 4+ while in Decatarai stance, or score Critical Wounds on a 4+ while in Rendax stance.
**Martial Philosopher: **This unit can use this ability before either player deploys their units on the battlefield. At this time, the player selects up to 5 friendly Adeptus Custodes Battleline units to gain 1 of the following abilities: Scouts 6”, Deep Strike, Infiltrators. A maximum of 3 of the 5 units can benefit from any 1 ability (for example, all 5 eligible units cannot gain the Infiltrators ability, only up to 3 of the 5 can).

This model can replace its Guardian Spear with Bolter with 1 of the following:
- Guardian Spear with Meltagun
- Castellan Axe with Bolter
- Sentinel Blade
