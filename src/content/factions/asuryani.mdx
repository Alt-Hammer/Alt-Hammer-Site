## Army Rules

##### Battle Focus


<span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-infantry" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Infantry</span> units (excluding units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="wraith" data-type="keyword">Wraith</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> keywords) possess 4 Activation Points.


##### Agile Maneuvers


<span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-infantry" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Infantry</span> units (excluding units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="wraith" data-type="keyword">Wraith</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> keywords) can choose to use this ability when Activated. When it does so, declare that the unit will use Agile Maneuvers. The unit can then use the <span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span> Action twice as part of that Activation. When a unit uses this ability, it cannot use the <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight </span>Action as part of that Activation, and cannot end any Move within 9” of any enemy models.


##### Asuryani Psychic Powers


<span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-psykers" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Psykers </span>are highly trained masters of the Warp. These <span class="keyword" data-term="psykers" data-type="keyword">Psykers</span> Cast Psychic Powers to enhance the combat effectiveness of their <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> cohort or disrupt their enemies and expose them to swift judgement by friendly units.

Each time an <span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-psyker" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Psyker</span> is Activated, you can choose any number of these abilities as specified in its Unit Profile to Cast as part of its Activation. To do so, complete a Psychic Test as normal, applying any modifiers as applicable.

Unless otherwise stated, the effects of a Psychic Power lasts until the end of the Battle Round.


###### Doom


Select 1 enemy unit within 18” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span>. That unit reduces its Leadership characteristic by 2.


###### Guide


Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span>. That unit increases its Ballistic Skill or Weapons Skill by 1 (select 1).


###### Jinx


Select 1 enemy unit within 18” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span> and 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the Psyker. The enemy unit reduces its Hit rolls by 1 when making Ranged attacks against the selected friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit.


###### Fortune


Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker. </span>That unit increases its Armour Save characteristic by 1.


###### Fog of Dreams


Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker. </span>That unit gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="stealth" data-type="keyword">Stealth</span> ability.


###### Branching Fates


Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani" data-type="keyword">Asuryani</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span> that has not yet been Activated this Battle Round. That unit can turn up to D3+1 Hit rolls of a 1 into Critical Hits as part of the next Action that involves making attacks.


###### Tears of Isha


Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="wraith" data-type="keyword">Wraith</span> unit within 3” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span>. That unit can restore 1 destroyed model to the battlefield, or restore up to D3+1 lost wounds to 1 of its models.


###### Embolden


Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari-infantry" data-type="keyword">Aeldari Infantry</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker. </span>Models in that unit increases their Leadership characteristic by 1 and Objective Control value by 1 until the end of your next Command Phase.


## Detachment Traits

Your Asuryani army can select 1 of the following Detachment Traits. These detachment traits offer Force Organization and army structure changes to represent various Aeldari Craftworlds and their warfare styles on the tabletop. 


###### Windriders Host	

- Windriders and Vypers gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- Guardians lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- <span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani" data-type="keyword">Asuryani</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> units gain access to the Battle Focus and Agile Maneuvers abilities, but can only move up to a value in inches equal to a unit’s lowest Initiative value when using the Agile Maneuvers ability.

###### Wraith Host	

- Wraithguard gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- Guardians lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- <span class="keyword" data-term="wraith" data-type="keyword">Wraith</span> units gain access to the Battle Focus and Agile Maneuvers abilities

###### Star Host	

- Guardians lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- <span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani" data-type="keyword">Asuryani</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="vehicles" data-type="keyword">Vehicles</span> gain access to the Battle Focus and Agile Maneuvers abilities, but can only move up to a value in inches equal to a unit’s lowest Initiative value when using the Agile Maneuvers ability.
- <span class="keyword" data-term="every-asuryani" data-type="keyword">Every Asuryani</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry-unit-in-your-army-must-start-the-battle-in-a-transport" data-type="keyword">Infantry unit in your army must start the battle in a Transport</span>

###### Aspect Host


Select 1 of the following <span class="keyword" data-term="aspect-warriors" data-type="keyword">Aspect Warriors</span> units to gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword:
- Dire Avengers
- Dark Reapers
- Fire Dragons
- Howling Banshees
- Striking Scorpions
- Swooping Hawks
- Warp Spiders
- Shining Spears

Further, your army:
- Must include an Autarch with an Autarch Path that allows it to Lead the <span class="keyword" data-term="aspect-warriors" data-type="keyword">Aspect Warriors</span> unit you selected to gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword.
- Can only include Aeldari units with the following keywords: <span class="keyword" data-term="aspect-warriors" data-type="keyword">Aspect Warriors</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="transport-x" data-type="keyword">Transport [X]</span>


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Shimmershield	25 Points


The bearer’s unit gains the following abilities while the bearer is on the battlefield:
- The bearer is considered to have a Toughness characteristic of 5 against Ranged attacks.
- Increase Wounds characteristic of models in the unit by 1
- Add the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability

###### Scattershield	30 Points


The bearer increases its Wounds characteristic by 3 and gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability. 


###### Jetbike	20 Points

- Increase Movement characteristic to 14”
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 2
- Increase Wounds characteristic by 2
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span>, <span class="keyword" data-term="fly-hover" data-type="keyword">Fly, Hover </span>and <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike </span>abilities

###### Twin-linked Weapon	Varies (see below)


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 

Twin-linked weapons have 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 15 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 3 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 8 points for all other weapons.

###### Banshee Mask	5 Points


Enemy units with models in Base-to-Base Contact with the bearer reduce their Leadership characteristic by 1.


###### Force Shield	12 Points


The bearer:
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increases its Wounds characteristic by 1
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability
