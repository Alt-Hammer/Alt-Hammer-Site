## Army Rules

##### Battle Focus

<span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-infantry" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Infantry</span> units (excluding units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="wraith" data-type="keyword">Wraith</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> keywords) possess 4 Activation Points.

##### Agile Maneuvers

<span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-infantry" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Infantry</span> units (excluding units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="wraith" data-type="keyword">Wraith</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> keywords) can choose to use this ability when Activated. When it does so, declare that the unit will use Agile Maneuvers. The unit can then use the <span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span> Action twice as part of that Activation. When a unit uses this ability, it cannot use the <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight </span>Action as part of that Activation, and cannot end any Move within 9” of any enemy models.

##### Asuryani Psychic Powers

<span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-psykers" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Psykers </span>are highly trained masters of the Warp. These <span class="keyword" data-term="psykers" data-type="keyword">Psykers</span> Cast Psychic Powers to enhance the combat effectiveness of their <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> cohort or disrupt their enemies and expose them to swift judgement by friendly units.
Each time an <span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani-psyker" data-type="keyword">Asuryani Psyker</span> is Activated, you can choose any number of these abilities as specified in its Unit Profile to Cast as part of its Activation. To do so, complete a Psychic Test as normal, applying any modifiers as applicable.
Unless otherwise stated, the effects of a Psychic Power lasts until the end of the Battle Round.

###### Doom

Select 1 enemy unit within 18” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span>. That unit reduces its Leadership characteristic by 2.

###### Guide

Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span>. That unit increases its Ballistic Skill or Weapons Skill by 1 (select 1).

###### Jinx

Select 1 enemy unit within 18” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span> and 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the Psyker. The enemy unit reduces its Hit rolls by 1 when making Ranged attacks against the selected friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit.

###### Fortune

Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker. </span>That unit increases its Armour Save characteristic by 1.

###### Fog of Dreams

Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari" data-type="keyword">Aeldari</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker. </span>That unit gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="stealth" data-type="keyword">Stealth</span> ability.

###### Branching Fates

Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="asuryani" data-type="keyword">Asuryani</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span> that has not yet been Activated this Battle Round. That unit can turn up to D3+1 Hit rolls of a 1 into Critical Hits as part of the next Action that involves making attacks.

###### Tears of Isha

Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="wraith" data-type="keyword">Wraith</span> unit within 3” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span>. That unit can restore 1 destroyed model to the battlefield, or restore up to D3+1 lost wounds to 1 of its models.

###### Embolden

Select 1 friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="aeldari-infantry" data-type="keyword">Aeldari Infantry</span> unit within 6” of the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker. </span>Models in that unit increases their Leadership characteristic by 1 and Objective Control value by 1 until the end of your next Command Phase.


## Detachment Traits

Your Asuryani army can select 1 of the following Detachment Traits. These detachment traits offer force composition and army structure changes to represent various Aeldari Craftworlds and their warfare styles on the tabletop.
