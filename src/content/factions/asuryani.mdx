## Army Rules

##### Battle Focus {#battle-focus}

Asuryani Infantry units (excluding units with the Wraith or Mounted keywords) possess 4 Activation Points.

##### Agile Maneuvers {#agile-maneuvers}

Asuryani Infantry units (excluding units with the Wraith or Mounted keywords) can choose to use this ability when Activated. When it does so, declare that the unit will use Agile Maneuvers. The unit can then use the Move Action twice as part of that Activation. When a unit uses this ability, it cannot use the Charge and Fight Action as part of that Activation, and cannot end any Move within 9” of any enemy models.

##### Asuryani Psychic Powers {#asuryani-psychic-powers}

Asuryani Psykers are highly trained masters of the Warp. These Psykers Cast Psychic Powers to enhance the combat effectiveness of their Aeldari cohort or disrupt their enemies and expose them to swift judgement by friendly units.
Each time an Asuryani Psyker is Activated, you can choose any number of these abilities as specified in its Unit Profile to Cast as part of its Activation. To do so, complete a Psychic Test as normal, applying any modifiers as applicable.
Unless otherwise stated, the effects of a Psychic Power lasts until the end of the Battle Round.

###### Doom {#doom}

Select 1 enemy unit within 18” of the Psyker. That unit reduces its Leadership characteristic by 2.

###### Guide {#guide}

Select 1 friendly Aeldari unit within 6” of the Psyker. That unit increases its Ballistic Skill or Weapons Skill by 1 (select 1).

###### Jinx {#jinx}

Select 1 enemy unit within 18” of the Psyker and 1 friendly Aeldari unit within 6” of the Psyker. The enemy unit reduces its Hit rolls by 1 when making Ranged attacks against the selected friendly Aeldari unit.

###### Fortune {#fortune}

Select 1 friendly Aeldari unit within 6” of the Psyker. That unit increases its Armour Save characteristic by 1.

###### Fog of Dreams {#fog-of-dreams}

Select 1 friendly Aeldari unit within 6” of the Psyker. That unit gains the Stealth ability.

###### Branching Fates {#branching-fates}

Select 1 friendly Asuryani unit within 6” of the Psyker that has not yet been Activated this Battle Round. That unit can turn up to D3+1 Hit rolls of a 1 into Critical Hits as part of the next Action that involves making attacks.

###### Tears of Isha {#tears-of-isha}

Select 1 friendly Wraith unit within 3” of the Psyker. That unit can restore 1 destroyed model to the battlefield, or restore up to D3+1 lost wounds to 1 of its models.

###### Embolden {#embolden}

Select 1 friendly Aeldari Infantry unit within 6” of the Psyker. Models in that unit increases their Leadership characteristic by 1 and Objective Control value by 1 until the end of your next Command Phase.


## Detachment Traits

Your Asuryani army can select 1 of the following Detachment Traits. These detachment traits offer force composition and army structure changes to represent various Aeldari Craftworlds and their warfare styles on the tabletop. 

###### Windriders Host	 {#windriders-host}

- Windriders and Vypers gain the Battleline keyword
- Guardians lose the Battleline keyword
- Asuryani Mounted units gain access to the Battle Focus and Agile Maneuvers abilities

###### Wraith Host	 {#wraith-host}

- Wraithguard gain the Battleline keyword
- Guardians lose the Battleline keyword
- Wraith units gain access to the Battle Focus and Agile Maneuvers abilities

###### Star Host	 {#star-host}

- Guardians lose the Battleline keyword
- Asuryani Vehicles gain access to the Battle Focus and Agile Maneuvers abilities
- Every Asuryani Infantry unit in your army must start the battle in a Transport

###### Aspect Host {#aspect-host}

Select 1 of the following Aspect Warriors units to gain the Battleline keyword:
- Dire Avengers
- Dark Reapers
- Fire Dragons
- Howling Banshees
- Striking Scorpions
- Swooping Hawks
- Warp Spiders
- Shining Spears

Further, your army:
- Must include an Autarch with an Autarch Path that allows it to Lead the Aspect Warriors unit you selected to gain the Battleline keyword.
- Can only include Aeldari units with the following keywords: Aspect Warriors or Transport [X]

- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
