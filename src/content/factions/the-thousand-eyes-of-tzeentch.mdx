## Army Rules

##### Apparitions in Time and Space

During your Command Phase, you can use this ability. To do so, select up to 3 Tzeentch Infantry units for every 1,000 points and then select 1 of the following to be in effect for each of those units until the end of the Battle Round:
- Increase the unit's Activation Points by 1
- Gain the ability to use the Ascend to the Skies Action
- Increase the Movement characteristic of models in the unit by 1"
- Gain the Stealth ability
- Gain the Phase Shift ability
- Gain the Feel No Pain 5+ ability against Ranged attacks

Alternatively, select any eligible units before the start of the first Battle Round and declare that those units will be held in Strategic Reserves. Those units gain the Deep Strike ability.

##### Blessings of Tzeentch

When a friendly Tzeentch Infantry unit has a starting strength of 9 models:
- Increase the Leadership characteristic of models in the unit by 1
- Select up to 2 abilities from the **Apparitions in Space and Time** Army Rule to be in effect for the unit.
- Add 1 to the Objective Control value of models in that unit

##### Daemonhost

Up to 50% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the Chaos Daemons Faction, but these units must possess the Tzeentch keyword.
