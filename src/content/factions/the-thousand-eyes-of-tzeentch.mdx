## Army Rules

##### Apparitions in Time and Space


During your Command Phase, you can use this ability. To do so, select up to 3 <span class="keyword" data-term="tzeentch-infantry" data-type="keyword">Tzeentch Infantry</span> units for every 1,000 points and then select 1 of the following to be in effect for each of those units until the end of the Battle Round:
- Increase the unit's Activation Points by 1
- Gain the ability to use the <span class="keyword" data-term="ascend-to-the-skies" data-type="action">Ascend to the Skies</span> Action
- Increase the Movement characteristic of models in the unit by 1"
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="stealth" data-type="keyword">Stealth</span> ability
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="phase-shift" data-type="keyword">Phase Shift</span> ability
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span> ability against Ranged attacks

Alternatively, select any eligible units before the start of the first Battle Round and declare that those units will be held in Strategic Reserves. Those units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability.


##### Blessings of Tzeentch


When a friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="tzeentch-infantry" data-type="keyword">Tzeentch Infantry</span> unit has a starting strength of 9 models:
- Increase the Leadership characteristic of models in the unit by 1
- Select up to 2 abilities from the **Apparitions in Space and Time** Army Rule to be in effect for the unit.
- Add 1 to the Objective Control value of models in that unit

##### Daemonhost


Up to 50% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the Chaos Daemons Faction, but these units must possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="tzeentch" data-type="keyword">Tzeentch</span> keyword.
