## Army Rules

##### Synapse Control


Several Tyranids units possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="synapse-x" data-type="keyword">Synapse [X]”</span> keyword, allowing these units to control and enhance the resilience and use of lesser bioforms more common to a Tyranids army. The <span class="keyword" data-term="synapse-x" data-type="keyword">Synapse [X]”</span> keyword confers the following ability:


###### Synapse [X]”


This unit provides the following benefits to all <span class="keyword" data-term="tyranids" data-type="keyword">Tyranids</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="beast" data-type="keyword">Beast</span> units with at least one model within [X]” of this unit:
- Increase the Activation Points of the unit by 1
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="fearless" data-type="keyword">Fearless</span> ability

##### Shadow in the Warp


Enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="psykers" data-type="keyword">Psykers</span> must take a Battle Shock Test at the start of their Activation when they are wholly within [X]” of at least 1 Tyranid unit with the <span class="keyword" data-term="synapse-x" data-type="keyword">Synapse [X]”</span> ability. If the Battle Shock Test is failed, that enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span> is Battle Shocked.


## Detachment Traits

Your Tyranid army can select 1 of the following Detachment Traits. 


###### Endless Swarm

- Each time a <span class="keyword" data-term="tyranid-battleline-beast" data-type="keyword">Tyranid Battleline Beast</span> unit from your army is Activated, if that unit is within range of a unit with the Synapse [X]” ability, you can immediately activate 1 other Battleline unit of the same type on the battlefield. Both of these units count as a single Activation, but must complete the same Actions at the same time.
- Each time a <span class="keyword" data-term="tyranid-battleline-beast" data-type="keyword">Tyranid Battleline Beast</span> model from your army is destroyed, roll 1 D6. On a 5+ place that model into a group off of the table. This is your next Endless Swarm Wave. At the start of the next Battle Round, organize the models in your Endless Swarm Wave into units of matching models - 1 unit per model type (ie, 1 unit of Termagaunts, 1 unit of Hormagaunts), which are then considered to be in Strategic Reserves. These units must then be Activated (following the standard rules for activating units in Strategic Reserves) and must be placed anywhere on the battlefield so that it is wholly within 6” of any Battlefield Edge in your Deployment Zone.

###### Vanguard Invaders 


The following units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword: Genestealers, Lictor

The following units lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword: Termagaunts, Hormagaunts, Warriors


###### Crusher Stampede 


The following units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword: Carnifexes, Maleceptor 

The following units lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword: Termagaunts, Hormagaunts, Warriors


###### Aerial Scourge 


The following units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword: Gargoyles, Harpy

The following units lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword: Termagaunts, Hormagaunts, Warriors


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Adrenal Glands	10 Points


+2 to Initiative characteristic


###### Hardened Carapace	10 Points for T6 and below / 20 Points for T7 and above

- +3 to Wounds characteristic

###### Scything Wings	30 Points

- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="fly" data-type="keyword">Fly</span> keyword
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability 
- Includes <span class="weapon-ref" data-weapon="scything-wings">Scything Wings</span> Melee weapon

###### Serpentine Bioform	15 Points

- Cannot be equipped by models equipped with Scything Wings
- Increase Movement characteristic to 9”
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability

###### Neuroloids	5 Points


For each Neuroloid included with this unit, extend the Synapse Range of this unit by 6”.


###### Warp Field	15 Points


Models equipped with a Warp Field gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability. Additionally, once per Battle, models equipped with a Warp Field can attempt to enhance their Warp Field. To do so, take a Psychic Test. If the test is passed, the bearer gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-4" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 4+</span> ability until the end of the Battle Round.


###### Twin-Linked Weapon	Varies (see below)


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 

Twin-linked weapons select 1 of 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.
