## Army Rules

##### Power from Pain

Drukhari draw their strength from the anticipation and delivery of pain onto their prey. This ability is translated for the tabletop by gaining and spending Pain Tokens.

###### Gaining Pain Tokens

<span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> armies gain 1 Pain Token:
- At the start of the battle for every 500 Points in battle size.
- Each time an enemy unit is destroyed
- Each time an enemy unit fails a Battle-shock test 

Each time you gain a Pain Token, keep it near the tabletop. This is your Pain Token pool.

###### Spending Pain Tokens

When a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> unit is Activated you can choose to make that unit Empowered Through Pain by Spending 1 or more Pain Tokens. Each Pain Token spent in this manner confers 1 of the following abilities, which lasts until the end of the Battle Round:
- The unit gains 1 Activation Point
- The unit gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> ability
- The unit gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability
- The unit increases its Attacks characteristic by 2
- The unit increases its Strength characteristic by 2
- The unit scores Critical Hits on a 5+
- The unit scores Critical Wounds on a 5+

Each of these abilities can only be applied to a unit once per Battle Round, but any number of Pain Tokens can be spent on a single unit. For example, you cannot spend 2 Pain Tokens on 1 unit in 1 Battle Round to gain 2 additional Activation Points.

##### Realspace Raiders

<span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> are always considered to be the Attacker when determining the Mission that will be played.


## Detachment Traits

Unlike many factions, the Drukhari are not driven by territorial conquest or the need to defend their realm from outside invaders. Drukhari forces muster for battle most often simply for the sinister joy of practicing the art of death, torture, fleshcraft, or other such dark motives that can be realized against a target of opportunity. 
To represent this on the battlefield, Drukhari armies can select 1 of the following Detachment Abilities to function as a thematic, objectives-based gameplay imperative that lasts for the entire Battle.

###### Realspace Raiding Party

- For every enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="beast" data-type="keyword">Beast</span> model that is dragged off of the battlefield by a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> unit, gain 3 Victory Points and 1 Pain Token.
- For every enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="monster" data-type="keyword">Monster</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="vehicle" data-type="keyword">Vehicle</span> model that is dragged off of the battlefield by a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> unit, gain 8 Victory Points and 3 Pain Tokens.
- For every enemy model with the <span class="keyword" data-term="character" data-type="keyword">Character</span> keyword model that is dragged off of the battlefield by a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> unit, gain an additional 5 Victory Points and 3 Pain Tokens.
- Gain 2 Victory points and 1 Pain Token for each <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> unit that is removed from the battlefield while dragging an enemy unit.

Victory Points and Pain Token values identified above are doubled if the enemy models are dragged from the tabletop within their own Deployment Zone.
Enemy models are successfully dragged off the tabletop after a Drukhari unit Moves off of the tabletop with that enemy model (or models) whilst they are subject to the conditions of a Harpoon attack.

###### The Sublime Slaughtering

- For every 5 enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="beast" data-type="keyword">Beast</span> models destroyed by a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> Melee attack, gain 2 Victory Points and 1 Pain Token.
- For every enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="monster" data-type="keyword">Monster</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="vehicle" data-type="keyword">Vehicle</span> model destroyed by a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> Melee attack, gain 5 Victory Points and 3 Pain Tokens.
- For every enemy model with the <span class="keyword" data-term="character" data-type="keyword">Character</span> keyword model that is destroyed by a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> Melee attack, gain an additional 5 Victory Points and 3 Pain Tokens.

###### My Laboratory of Fleshcraft

This detachment ability can only be selected if a Haemonculus is your Warlord. 
- Attacks with the <span class="keyword" data-term="poison-x" data-type="keyword">Poison [X]</span> ability increase [X] by 1, but gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="hazardous-1" data-type="keyword">Hazardous 1</span> ability. If such attacks already possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="hazardous-x" data-type="keyword">Hazardous [X]</span> ability, increase [X] by 1.
- For every enemy unit destroyed by a <span class="keyword" data-term="drukhari" data-type="keyword">Drukhari</span> attack with the <span class="keyword" data-term="poison-x" data-type="keyword">Poison [X]</span> ability, gain 1 Victory Point and 1 Pain Token. If the destroyed model is a <span class="keyword" data-term="monster" data-type="keyword">Monster</span>, gain an additional 2 Victory Points and 1 Pain Token. If the destroyed model is a <span class="keyword" data-term="character" data-type="keyword">Character</span>, gain an additional 2 Victory Points and 1 Pain Token.

<span class="keyword" data-term="haemonculus-covens" data-type="keyword">Haemonculus Covens</span> units can use the <span class="keyword" data-term="mission-task" data-type="action">Mission Task</span> action to complete the following:
<span class="keyword" data-term="torturous-experiment" data-type="keyword">Torturous Experiment:</span> To be eligible to complete this Action, the unit must not have used - and cannot subsequently use - the <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span>, or <span class="keyword" data-term="fall-back" data-type="action">Fall Back</span> Actions as part of this Activation. To complete this Action, roll a D6:
- On a 4+, or a 2+ if the Action is being completed by a Haemonculus, the enemy unit suffers D3+1 Mortal Wounds and you gain 2 Victory Points and 1 Pain Token. 
- If the enemy unit is Battle Shocked, you gain 4 Victory Points and 2 Pain Tokens instead.
- Each enemy unit can only be the subject a Torturous Experiment Action once per Battle Round.


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Combat Drugs	30 Points

During your Command Phase, your unit can use Combat Drugs. To do so, select one of the following combat drug effects, which is in effect unit until the end of the Battle Round:
- Increase Movement characteristic by 2”
- Increase Initiative characteristic by 2
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> ability
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="fearless" data-type="keyword">Fearless</span> ability
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span> ability

Alternatively, you can choose to expend 1 Pain token per combat drug effect and have all of those in effect until the end of the Battle Round. However, when you do this, Weapons equipped by the unit gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="hazardous-3" data-type="keyword">Hazardous 3</span> ability.
