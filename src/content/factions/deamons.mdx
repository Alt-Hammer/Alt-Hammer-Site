## Army Rules

##### Empyrean Horrors

When an enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span>, <span class="keyword" data-term="beast" data-type="keyword">Beast</span> or <span class="keyword" data-term="monster" data-type="keyword">Monster</span> unit has at least 1 model within 6” of a <span class="keyword" data-term="chaos-daemons" data-type="keyword">Chaos Daemons</span> unit, that unit must immediately take a Battle Shock Test, subtracting 1 from its Leadership characteristic if the <span class="keyword" data-term="chaos-daemons" data-type="keyword">Chaos Daemons</span> unit is a <span class="keyword" data-term="monster" data-type="keyword">Monster</span>.
If the test is passed, the unit is not Battle Shocked and does not need to take future Battle Shock Tests caused by this Army Rule. 
If the test is failed, the unit is Battle Shocked, and must take future Battle Shock Tests caused by this Army Rule until the first time the unit passes a Battle Shock Test.
