## Army Rules

##### Inevitability of Ruin


Units with the <span class="keyword" data-term="imperium" data-type="keyword">Imperium</span> keyword reduce their Leadership characteristic by 1 when at least 1 model in that unit is within 6" of a unit with the <span class="keyword" data-term="chaos-undivided" data-type="keyword">Chaos Undivided</span> keyword.


##### Daemon Host


Up to 25% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the <span class="keyword" data-term="chaos" data-type="keyword">Chaos</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="daemons" data-type="keyword">Daemons</span> Faction. However, only a maximum of 2 <span class="keyword" data-term="chaos" data-type="keyword">Chaos</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="daemons" data-type="keyword">Daemons</span> units can be included in your army if they possess any of the following keywords: <span class="keyword" data-term="khorne-nurgle-slaanesh-tzeentch" data-type="keyword">Khorne, Nurgle, Slaanesh, Tzeentch</span>


##### Favoured of the Gods


Up to 25% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from other <span class="keyword" data-term="chaos" data-type="keyword">Chaos</span> factions, as follows:
- If your Warlord has the <span class="keyword" data-term="khorne" data-type="keyword">Khorne</span> keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Bloodbound of Khorne faction
- If your Warlord has the <span class="keyword" data-term="nurgle" data-type="keyword">Nurgle</span> keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Plaguebound of Nurgle faction
- If your Warlord has the <span class="keyword" data-term="slaanesh" data-type="keyword">Slaanesh</span> keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Velvet Choirs of Slaanesh faction
- If your Warlord has the <span class="keyword" data-term="tzeentch" data-type="keyword">Tzeentch</span> keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Thousand Eyes of Tzeentch faction

If your Warlord only has the <span class="keyword" data-term="undivided" data-type="keyword">Undivided</span> Keyword, up to 1 unit for every 2000 points of battle size can be taken from each of the factions listed above.


##### Dread Lance


If your Warlord possesses the <span class="keyword" data-term="questor-traitoris" data-type="keyword">Questor Traitoris</span> keyword:
- War Dogs gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword
- Legionaries and Cultists lose the <span class="keyword" data-term="battleline" data-type="keyword">Battleline</span> keyword

Up to 100% of the total points value of your army can be spent on the following <span class="keyword" data-term="astra-militarum" data-type="keyword">Astra Militarum</span> units:

<span class="keyword" data-term="astra-militarum-infantry" data-type="keyword">Astra Militarum Infantry</span> units from the list above can make 1 selection from the Gifts of Chaos.


### Gifts of Chaos


Many <span class="keyword" data-term="chaos-undivided" data-type="keyword">Chaos Undivided</span> units can select options from the Gifts of Chaos below. Unless otherwise stated, this selection applies to every model in the unit at the per-model points cost detailed below. Note that Gifts of Chaos have 2 per-model points costs: the lower points cost is for models with a Toughness characteristic of 6 or less, and the higher points cost for models with a Toughness characteristic of 7 or higher.


<h6 id="mark-of-khorne">Mark of Khorne<span class="heading-value">5 points / 15 points</span></h6>

- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="khorne" data-type="keyword">Khorne</span> keyword
- Add 1" to Movement characteristic
- Add 1 to Attacks characteristic

<h6 id="mark-of-nurgle">Mark of Nurgle<span class="heading-value">5 points / 15 points</span></h6>

- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="nurgle" data-type="keyword">Nurgle</span> keyword
- Add 1 to Toughness characteristic
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-6" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 6+</span> ability

<h6 id="mark-of-tzeentch">Mark of Tzeentch<span class="heading-value">4 points / 12 points</span></h6>

- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="tzeentch" data-type="keyword">Tzeentch</span> keyword
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="infiltrators" data-type="keyword">Infiltrators</span> ability
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="unyielding" data-type="keyword">Unyielding</span> ability

<h6 id="mark-of-slaanesh">Mark of Slaanesh<span class="heading-value">5 points / 15 points</span></h6>

- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="slaanesh" data-type="keyword">Slaanesh</span> keyword
- Add 1 to Weapons Skill characteristic
- Add 1 to Initiative characteristic


## Wargear Upgrades

#### Heretic Astartes Armour


The majority of Heretic Astartes units are equipped with Tacticus armour. However, some Heretic Units are equipped with 1 of the following armour options instead. Additionally, some Heretic Astartes units are eligible to replace their Tacticus armour with 1 of the following Heretic Astartes Armour options. The abilities of these armour patterns are detailed here. Every model in a unit must be equipped with the same armour type at the points cost detailed below, and players should be aware that not all weapon options are available for every armour type. This is identified by weapon in the Adeptus Astartes Weapons Data Table.


<h6 id="tacticus-armour">Tacticus Armour<span class="heading-value">0 points</span></h6>


Many <span class="keyword" data-term="heretic-astartes-infantry" data-type="keyword">Heretic Astartes Infantry</span> models are equipped with Tacticus Armour by default and possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="tacticus" data-type="keyword">Tacticus</span> keyword.


<h6 id="raptor-armour">Raptor Armour<span class="heading-value">10 points</span></h6>

- Remove the <span class="keyword" data-term="tacticus" data-type="keyword">Tacticus</span> keyword
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="raptor" data-type="keyword">Raptor</span> keyword
- Increase Movement characteristic to 12"
- Increase Initiative characteristic by 1
- Increase Attacks characteristic by 1
- Reduce Wounds characteristic by 1
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability
- Can only be equipped with weapons 

<h6 id="havoc-armour">Havoc Armour<span class="heading-value">12 points</span></h6>

- Remove the <span class="keyword" data-term="tacticus" data-type="keyword">Tacticus</span> keyword
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="havoc" data-type="keyword">Havoc</span> keyword
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1"
- Reduce Initiative characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increase Wounds Characteristic by 1
- Ignore the effects of the <span class="keyword" data-term="heavy" data-type="keyword">Heavy</span> keyword when making attacks
- Model must be on a 40mm base

<h6 id="terminator-armour">Terminator Armour<span class="heading-value">20 Points</span></h6>

- Remove the <span class="keyword" data-term="tacticus" data-type="keyword">Tacticus</span> keyword
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="havoc" data-type="keyword">Havoc</span> keyword
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1"
- Reduce Initiative characteristic by 1
- Increase Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increase Wounds Characteristic by 1
- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability
- Ignore the effects of the <span class="keyword" data-term="heavy" data-type="keyword">Heavy</span> keyword when making attacks
- Model must be on a 40mm to 60mm base
- Model counts as 2 models for the purposes of the <span class="keyword" data-term="transport-x" data-type="keyword">Transport [X]</span> ability

<h6 id="twin-linked">Twin-linked<span class="heading-value">Varies (see below)</span></h6>


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit's datasheet. The points cost for the Twin-linked upgrade varies, as described below. 

This upgrade costs 18 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more

This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower

The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.


<h6 id="dozer-blade">Dozer Blade<span class="heading-value">20 Points</span></h6>


Models equipped with a Dozer Blade improve their Breach Test roll by 1 and do not reduce their Movement characteristic by 2" when moving through a terrain feature.

Additionally, models equipped with a Dozer Blade modify their Melee attack as follows:
- Increase Attacks characteristic by 3
- Increase Strength characteristic by 2
- Increase Armour Piercing characteristic by 1 (ie, AP 0 attacks become AP -1)
- Increase Damage characteristic by 1

<h6 id="smoke-launchers">Smoke Launchers<span class="heading-value">10 Points</span></h6>


The bearer can make 2 attacks with <span class="weapon-ref" data-weapon="smoke-grenades">Smoke Grenades</span> as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span> action.


<h6 id="ion-shield">Ion Shield<span class="heading-value">55 Points</span></h6>


The bearer gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> against Ranged attacks. 

Ranged attacks made against a model equipped with an Ion Shield reduce their:
- Strength characteristic by 3
- Armour Piercing characteristic by 2
- Damage Characteristic by half (rounding up)

The Ion Shield has no effect against attacks (Ranged or Melee) made against the bearer by models in Base-to-Base Contact with the bearer.
