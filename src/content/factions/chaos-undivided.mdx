## Army Rules

##### Inevitability of Ruin {#inevitability-of-ruin}

Units with the Imperium keyword reduce their Leadership characteristic by 1 when at least 1 model in that unit is within 6” of a unit with the Chaos Undivided keyword.

##### Daemon Host {#daemon-host}

Up to 25% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the Chaos Daemons Faction. However, only a maximum of 2 Chaos Daemons units can be included in your army if they possess any of the following keywords: Khorne, Nurgle, Slaanesh, Tzeentch

##### Favoured of the Gods {#favoured-of-the-gods}

Up to 25% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from other Chaos factions, as follows:
- If your Warlord has the Khorne keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Bloodbound of Khorne faction
- If your Warlord has the Nurgle keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Plaguebound of Nurgle faction
- If your Warlord has the Slaanesh keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Velvet Choirs of Slaanesh faction
- If your Warlord has the Tzeentch keyword, the full 25% of your army can be spent on units from The Thousand Eyes of Tzeentch faction

If your Warlord only has the Undivided Keyword, up to 1 unit for every 2000 points of battle size can be taken from each of the factions listed above.




##### Dread Lance {#dread-lance}

If your Warlord possesses the Questor Traitoris keyword:
- War Dogs gain the Battleline keyword
- Legionaries and Cultists lose the Battleline keyword


##### Traitor Militia {#traitor-militia}

Up to 100% of the total points value of your army can be spent on the following Astra Militarum units:

When including these units, they gain the Chaos keyword, but do not benefit from this faction’s Army Rules. Instead, you gain the ability to use the **Voice of Command** and **Regimental Coordination Army** Rules available to the Astra Militarum faction with these units. Regimental Traits available to the Astra Militarum faction are not available to units that possess the Chaos keyword.
Astra Militarum Infantry units from the list above can make 1 selection from the Gifts of Chaos.


### Gifts of Chaos {#gifts-of-chaos}

Many Chaos Undivided units offer the option to select options from the Gifts of Chaos below. Unless otherwise stated, this selection applies to every model in the unit at the per-model points cost detailed below. Note that Gifts of Chaos have 2 per-model points costs: the lower points cost is for models with a Toughness characteristic of 6 or less, and the higher points cost for models with a Toughness characteristic of 7 or higher.

###### Mark of Khorne	5 points / 15 points {#mark-of-khorne-5-points-15-points}

- Gain the Khorne keyword
- Add 1” to Movement characteristic
- Add 1 to Attacks characteristic

###### Mark of Nurgle	5 points / 15 points {#mark-of-nurgle-5-points-15-points}

- Gain the Nurgle keyword
- Add 1 to Toughness characteristic
- Gain the Feel No Pain 6+ ability

###### Mark of Tzeentch	4 points / 12 points {#mark-of-tzeentch-4-points-12-points}

- Gain the Tzeentch keyword
- Gain the Infiltrators ability
- Gain the Unyielding ability

###### Mark of Slaanesh	5 points / 15 points {#mark-of-slaanesh-5-points-15-points}

- Gain the Slaanesh keyword
- Add 1 to Weapons Skill characteristic
- Add 1 to Initiative characteristic


## Wargear Upgrades

#### Heretic Astartes Armour {#heretic-astartes-armour}

The majority of Heretic Astartes units are equipped with Tacticus armour. However, some Heretic Units are equipped with 1 of the following armour options instead. Additionally, some Heretic Astartes units are eligible to replace their Tacticus armour with 1 of the following Heretic Astartes Armour options. The abilities of these armour patterns are detailed here. Every model in a unit must be equipped with the same armour type at the points cost detailed below, and players should be aware that not all weapon options are available for every armour type. This is identified by weapon in the Adeptus Astartes Weapons Data Table.


###### Raptor Armour	10 points {#raptor-armour-10-points}

- Remove the Tacticus keyword
- Gain the Raptor keyword
- Increase Movement characteristic to 12”
- Increase Initiative characteristic by 1
- Increase Attacks characteristic by 1
- Reduce Wounds characteristic by 1
- Gain the Deep Strike ability
- Can only be equipped with weapons 

###### Havoc Armour	12 points {#havoc-armour-12-points}

- Remove the Tacticus keyword
- Gain the Havoc keyword
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1”
- Reduce Initiative characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increase Wounds Characteristic by 1
- Ignore the effects of the Heavy keyword when making attacks
- Model must be on a 40mm base

###### Terminator Armour	20 Points {#terminator-armour-20-points}

- Remove the Tacticus keyword
- Gain the Havoc keyword
- Reduce Movement characteristic by 1”
- Reduce Initiative characteristic by 1
- Increase Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increase Wounds Characteristic by 1
- Increase Armour Save characteristic to 2+
- Gain the Invulnerable Save 5+ ability
- Ignore the effects of the Heavy keyword when making attacks
- Model must be on a 40mm to 60mm base
- Model counts as 2 models for the purposes of the Transport [X] ability


#### Chaos Undivided Upgrades {#chaos-undivided-upgrades}


###### Accursed Weapon	10 points per Weapon {#accursed-weapon-10-points-per-weapon}

- Increase weapon’s Attacks characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Strength characteristic by 1
- Increase weapon’s Damage characteristic by 1

###### Dark Blessing	20 Points {#dark-blessing-20-points}

Models equipped with a Dark Blessing:
- Increase their Toughness characteristic by 2 against Ranged attacks
- Gain the Invulnerable Save 4+ ability.

###### Chaos Icon	30 points {#chaos-icon-30-points}

- Add 20 to the Objective Control value of the bearer whilst positioned wholly within range of a Primary Objective Marker
- Once per battle, during your Command Phase, select 1 Gift of Chaos not equipped by the unit. The effects of that Gift of Chaos are in effect until the end of the Battle Round.

###### Bike	20 points per model {#bike-20-points-per-model}

- Can only be selected by models equipped with Tacticus armour
- Increase Movement characteristic to 12”
- Increase Toughness characteristic by 1
- Add the Mounted* *keyword
- Add a Twin-Linked Boltgun to the model’s wargear
- Models equipped with a Bike must be on a 90mm x 52mm oval base or similar

###### Helstalker	80 points {#helstalker-80-points}

- Should be on a 120mmx95mm oval base
- Increase Movement characteristic to 10”
- Increase Strength characteristic to 6
- Increase Toughness characteristic to 6
- Increase Wounds characteristic to 8
- Gain the Mounted keyword
- Gain the Feel No Pain 5+ ability
- Add Scything Talons 
- Must select 1 of the following Wargear options:
- Magma Cutter
- Toxinjector
- Must select 1 of the following Wargear options:
- Accursed Autocannon
- Accursed Heavy Flamer

###### Combi-Bolter	15 points {#combi-bolter-15-points}

Models equipped with a Boltgun can upgrade that Boltgun to a Combi-bolter that must include 1 of the following Ranged weapons:
- Boltgun
- Flamer
- Meltagun
- Plasma Gun

###### Twin-linked Weapon	Varies (see below) {#twin-linked-weapon-varies-see-below}

Weapon gains the Twin-linked ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 
Twin-linked weapons have 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.

###### Dozer Blade	20 Points {#dozer-blade-20-points}

Models equipped with a Dozer Blade improve their Breach Test roll by 1 and do not reduce their Movement characteristic by 2” when moving through a terrain feature.
Additionally, models equipped with a Dozer Blade modify their Melee attack as follows:
- Increase Attacks characteristic by 3
- Increase Strength characteristic by 2
- Increase Armour Piercing characteristic by 1 (ie, AP 0 attacks become AP -1)
- Increase Damage characteristic by 1

###### Smoke Launchers	10 Points {#smoke-launchers-10-points}

The bearer can make 2 attacks with Smoke Grenades as part of a Shoot action.

###### Ion Shield	55 Points {#ion-shield-55-points}

The bearer gains the Invulnerable Save 5+ against Ranged attacks. 
Ranged attacks made against a model equipped with an Ion Shield reduce their:
- Strength characteristic by 3
- Armour Piercing characteristic by 2
- Damage Characteristic by half (rounding up)

The Ion Shield has no effect against attacks (Ranged or Melee) made against the bearer by models in Base-to-Base Contact with the bearer.
This model can replace its Accursed Ruinblade with an Accursed Daemonhammer.
This model can be equipped with Accursed Rending Claws.
This model can be equipped with Scything Wings.

###### Special Abilities {#special-abilities}

**Ascent to Greatness:** This model scores Critical Hits on a 5+. Once per Battle during your Command Phase, you can choose to improve this ability until the end of that Battle Round. When you do so, select 1 of the following to be in effect: 
- Every successful Hit is considered a Critical Hit 
- This model now scores Critical Wounds on a 4+ 

**Join Me in Glory****: **Each Battle Round, during your Command Phase, select 1 Chaos Infantry unit with at least 1 model within 3” of this model. That unit gains the Bodyguard ability for this model when this model is the subject of an enemy attack until the end of the Battle Round. However, that unit must have at least 1 model within 3” of this model for the duration of the Battle Round in order for this ability to be in effect.
**The Prize is Ours:** While this model is wholly within range of a Primary Objective Marker in No Mans Land or the enemy’s Deployment Zone:
- It is considered to have an Objective Control Value of 20.

###### Gifts of Chaos {#gifts-of-chaos}

This model can select 1 Gift of Chaos.

- Gain the Hazardous D3 ability if you select 2 Gifts of Chaos. 
- Gain the Hazardous D3+2 ability if you select 3 Gifts of Chaos, and the Hazardous [X] ability is triggered by any unsuccessful Hit roll.
- Gain the Hazardous D3+4 ability if you select 4 Gifts of Chaos, and the Hazardous [X] ability is triggered by any unsuccessful Hit roll.
