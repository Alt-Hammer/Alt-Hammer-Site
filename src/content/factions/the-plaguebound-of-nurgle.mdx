## Army Rules

##### Spread of Contagion

Enemy Infantry, Beasts and Monsters within 3" of a friendly Nurgle unit reduce their Toughness characteristic by 1 against Melee attacks.

##### Blessings of Nurgle

When a friendly Nurgle Infantry unit has a starting strength of 7 models:
- Increase the Leadership characteristic of models in the unit by 1
- Increase the range of the unit's Spread of Contagion ability to 5"
- Add 1 to the Objective Control value of models in that unit

##### Daemonhost

Up to 50% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the Chaos Daemons Faction, but these units must possess the Nurgle keyword.
