## Army Rules

##### Spread of Contagion

Enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span>, <span class="keyword" data-term="beasts" data-type="keyword">Beasts</span> and <span class="keyword" data-term="monsters" data-type="keyword">Monsters</span> within 3” of a friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="nurgle" data-type="keyword">Nurgle</span> unit reduce their Toughness characteristic by 1 against Melee attacks.

##### Blessings of Nurgle

When a friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="nurgle-infantry" data-type="keyword">Nurgle Infantry</span> unit has a starting strength of 7 models:
- Increase the Leadership characteristic of models in the unit by 1
- Increase the range of the unit’s Spread of Contagion ability to 5”
- Add 1 to the Objective Control value of models in that unit

##### Daemonhost

Up to 50% of the total points value of your army can be spent on units from the Chaos Daemons Faction, but these units must possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="nurgle" data-type="keyword">Nurgle</span> keyword.
