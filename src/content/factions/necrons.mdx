## Army Rules

##### Necrodermis


Each time a <span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> model is destroyed and removed from the battlefield, roll 1 D6. On a 5+, that model is resurrected and returned to the battlefield at the start of the next Battle Round during your Command Phase. Models returned to the battlefield in this manner have 1 Wound. It is recommended to keep your destroyed models in two groups for convenience: one group for models that will be resurrected, and one for models that will not (because the 4+ roll for that model was unsuccessful).


##### Command Protocols


<span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> units with at least 1 model within 12” of a <span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> use that <span class="keyword" data-term="leaders" data-type="keyword">Leader’s</span> Leadership value instead of their own for Battle Shock Tests that their unit must make.


## Wargear Upgrades

###### Form of Supreme Nobility	50 Points


This upgrade can only be taken by your Warlord. This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic by 2”
- Increases its Weapons Skill by 2
- Increases its Ballistics Skill by 2
- Increases its Initiative characteristic by 2
- Increases its Strength characteristic by 2
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increases its Wounds characteristic by 3
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> ability
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="fearless" data-type="keyword">Fearless</span> ability
- Must have a base size between 40mm to 60mm

###### Noble of the Lokhust Destroyer Cult	25 Points


This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic by 3”
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 2
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="mounted" data-type="keyword">Mounted</span> keyword
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="hover" data-type="keyword">Hover</span> keyword
- Must be modelled such that it physically resembles a Lokhust Destroyer model
- Must have a base size between 40mm to 60mm
- Gains the ability to - and can only - lead Lokhust Destroyers

Cannot be equipped by a model equipped any of the following: Noble of the Skorpek Destroyer Cult, Mantle of Living Metal.


###### Noble of the Skorpek Destroyer Cult	25 Points


This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic by 2”
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 2
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> ability
- Must be modelled such that it physically resembles a Skorpek Destroyer model
- Must have a 60mm base 
- Gains the ability to - and can only - lead Skorpek Destroyers

Cannot be equipped by a model equipped any of the following: Noble of the Lokhust Destroyer Cult, Mantle of Living Metal.


###### Noble of the Ophydian Destroyer Cult	25 Points


This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic by 4”
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 2
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> ability
- Must be modelled such that it physically resembles an Ophydian Destroyer model
- Must have a 50-60mm base 
- Gains the ability to - and can only - lead Skorpek Destroyers

Cannot be equipped by a model equipped any of the following: Noble of the Lokhust Destroyer Cult, Mantle of Living Metal.


###### Mantle of Living Metal	25 Points


Models equipped with a Mantle of Living Metal:
- Add 2 to their Wounds characteristic
- Gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability
- Successfully use the Necrodermis ability on a 2+ instead of a 5+

Cannot be equipped by a model equipped any of the following: Noble of the Lokhust Destroyer Cult, Noble of the Skorpek Destroyer Cult.


###### Resurrection Orb	30 Points


While a model equipped with a Resurrection Orb is leading a unit, resurrecting a destroyed model with the Necrodermis ability is successful on a 4+ instead of a 5+.


###### Chronometron	15 Points


While a model equipped with a Chronometron is leading a unit, once per Battle Round, that unit can <span class="keyword" data-term="move" data-type="action">Move</span> immediately after being declared the target of an enemy Ranged attack.


###### Phase Shifter	10 Points per Model


Models equipped with a Phase Shifter gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="phase-shift" data-type="keyword">Phase Shift</span> ability.


###### Nightmare Shroud	25 Points


Enemy units within 6” of this model reduce their Leadership characteristic to 9+.


###### Veil of Darkness	30 Points


While a model equipped with a Veil of Darkness is leading a unit, that unit has access to the <span class="keyword" data-term="ascend-to-the-skies" data-type="action">Ascend to the Skies</span> action and the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability.


###### Nebuloscope	20 Points


Ranged attacks by models equipped with a Nebuloscope - and by models being led by a model equipped with a Nebuloscope - have the Ignores Cover ability. Weapons equipped by the unit that already possess the Ignores Cover ability instead add 1 to their Hit roles.


###### Dispersion Shield	15 Points


Models equipped with a Dispersion Shield gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability and increase their Toughness characteristic by 3 against Ranged attacks.


###### Gloom Prism	10 Points


Friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> units with models that are within 6” of a model equipped with a Gloom Prism gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span> ability.


###### Twin-Linked Weapon	Varies (see below)	


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit’s datasheet. 

Twin-linked weapons have 3 points costs, based on the following: 
- This upgrade costs 20 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more
- This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower
- The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.

**My Will Be Done:** During your Command Phase, select up to 3 enemy units to be the target of this ability, and up to 3 friendly units to benefit from this ability. Select 1 of the following abilities that those friendly unit(s) will benefit from when making attacks against the selected enemy unit(s) the duration of the Battle Round:

- Friendly units score Critical Wounds on a 3+ against the selected targets
- Friendly units gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="precision" data-type="keyword">Precision</span> ability on the selected targets if that target possesses the Character keyword. 

If you choose to select only 1 enemy unit as the target of this ability, your selected friendly units benefit from all of the above abilities.

###### Force Organization

###### Unit Composition


1 Shard of Transcendent C’Tan


###### Harnessed God


Each Shard of Transcendent C’Tan is a shard of a specific god harnessed by a Necron noble to do their bidding. This unit must select 1 of the following Harnessed Gods to be a Shard of, which determines its Wargear and Abilities as described.


####### Shard of the Deceiver


Wargear: 

- This model is equipped with Deceiver’s Grace.

Abilities:

**Temporal Illusion: **Once per Battle Round, when this model is selected as the subject of a Ranged attack or a <span class="keyword" data-term="charge-and-fight" data-type="action">Charge and Fight</span> Action, remove the model from the tabletop and immediately place it back on the tabletop anywhere that is more than 12” from its original position, and not within 6” of any enemy models.


####### Shard of the Nightbringer


Wargear: 

- This model is equipped with Gaze of Death and the Scythe of the Nightbringer.

Abilities:

**The Reaping: **Once per battle round, select 1 enemy unit anytime during your activation for this unit that is within 6” of this model and which has no models with a Toughness characteristic higher than 5. Roll a D6. On a 2, up to 3 models from that unit are destroyed. On a 3-5, up to D3+3 models from that unit are destroyed. On a 5, up to D6+1 models from that unit are destroyed. On a 6, up to D6+4 models from that unit are destroyed.


####### Shard of the Void Dragon


Wargear: 

- This model is equipped with the Void Dragon’s Spear.

Abilities:

**Matter Absorption: **At the start of your activation for this unit, select one enemy <span class="keyword" data-term="vehicle" data-type="keyword">Vehicle</span> within 12" of this model and roll one D6. On a 2+, that enemy unit suffers D3+2 mortal wounds and this model regains that many lost wounds (or as many as it can get until it returns to Full Strength)**.**


####### Unformed Shard


Abilities:

**Untethered God: **At the start of each Battle Round, roll a D6. On a 1, this unit cannot be activated this Battle Round**. **On a 2, this model assumes the Wargear and Abilities of the Shard of the Deceiver until the end of the Battle Round. On a 3, this model assumes the Wargear and Abilities of the Shard of the Nightbringer until the end of the Battle Round. On a 4, this model assumes the Wargear and Abilities of the Shard of the Void Dragon until the end of the Battle Round. On a 5, you select which Harnessed God this model will be a Shard of until the end of the Battle Round. On a 6, this unit is destroyed and every unit within 12” of it takes D6 Mortal Wounds.
