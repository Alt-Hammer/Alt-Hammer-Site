## Army Rules

##### Necrodermis


Each time a <span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> model is destroyed and removed from the battlefield, roll 1 D6. On a 5+, that model is resurrected and returned to the battlefield at the start of the next Battle Round during your Command Phase. Models returned to the battlefield in this manner have 1 Wound. It is recommended to keep your destroyed models in two groups for convenience: one group for models that will be resurrected, and one for models that will not (because the 5+ roll for that model was unsuccessful).


##### Command Protocols


<span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> units with at least 1 model within 12" of a <span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="leader" data-type="keyword">Leader</span> use that <span class="keyword" data-term="leaders" data-type="keyword">Leader's</span> Leadership value instead of their own for Battle Shock Tests that their unit must make.


## Wargear Upgrades

<h6 id="form-of-supreme-nobility">Form of Supreme Nobility<span class="heading-value">75 Points</span></h6>


This upgrade can only be taken by your Warlord. This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic by 2"
- Increases its Weapons Skill by 2
- Increases its Ballistics Skill by 2
- Increases its Initiative characteristic by 2
- Increases its Strength characteristic by 2
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 1
- Increases its Wounds characteristic by 3
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="relentless" data-type="keyword">Relentless</span> ability
- Gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="fearless" data-type="keyword">Fearless</span> ability
- Must have a base size between 40mm to 60mm

<h6 id="command-barge">Command Barge<span class="heading-value">70 Points</span></h6>


This upgrade can only be taken by your Warlord. This model:
- Increases its Movement characteristic to 10"
- Increases its Strength characteristic by 1
- Increases its Toughness characteristic by 3
- Increases its Wounds characteristic by 5
- Gains the Vehicle keyword
- Gains the Hover keyword
- Must select 1 of the following: 1 <span class="weapon-ref" data-weapon="gauss-cannon">Gauss Cannon</span>, 1 <span class="weapon-ref" data-weapon="tesla-cannon">Tesla Cannon</span>

When equipped with a Command Barge, a Necron Lord cannot Lead any other Necron units. However, a Necron Lord equipped with a Command Barge increases the number of friendly and enemy units eligible to be selected as part of the **My Will Be Done** ability from 3 to 5.

Cannot be equipped by a model equipped any of the following: Form of Supreme Nobility, Noble of the Skorpek Destroyer Cult, Noble of the Ophydian Destroyer Cult, Noble of the Ophydian Destroyer Cult, Mantle of Living Metal.


<h6 id="noble-of-the-lokhust-destroyer-cult">Noble of the Lokhust Destroyer Cult<span class="heading-value">25 Points</span></h6>

Weapons equipped by the unit that already possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="ignores-cover" data-type="keyword">Ignores Cover</span> ability instead add 1 to their Hit roles.


<h6 id="dispersion-shield">Dispersion Shield<span class="heading-value">15 Points</span></h6>


Models equipped with a Dispersion Shield gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="invulnerable-save-5" data-type="keyword">Invulnerable Save 5+</span> ability and increase their Toughness characteristic by 3 against Ranged attacks.


<h6 id="gloom-prism">Gloom Prism<span class="heading-value">10 Points</span></h6>


Friendly <span class="keyword" data-term="necron" data-type="keyword">Necron</span> <span class="keyword" data-term="infantry" data-type="keyword">Infantry</span> units with models that are within 6" of a model equipped with a Gloom Prism gain the <span class="keyword" data-term="feel-no-pain-5" data-type="keyword">Feel No Pain 5+</span> ability.


<h6 id="twin-linked">Twin-linked<span class="heading-value">Varies (see below)</span></h6>


Weapon gains the <span class="keyword" data-term="twin-linked" data-type="keyword">Twin-linked</span> ability. This wargear upgrade will be identified for specific eligible weapons under the Weapon Options section of a unit's datasheet. The points cost for the Twin-linked upgrade varies, as described below. 

This upgrade costs 18 points for weapons that meet either of the following criteria: 
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 9 or greater
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 6 or more

This upgrade costs 5 points for weapons that meet all of the following criteria:
- The weapon has a Strength characteristic of 4 or lower
- The weapon has a minimum Attacks characteristic of 3 or lower

The upgrade costs 10 points for all other weapons.

<h6 id="standard-wargear">Standard Wargear</h6>


None.


<h6 id="abilities">Abilities</h6>


**Self-Destruct Protocol: **Each time a model from this unit is in base-to-base contact with an enemy unit, you can choose to use this ability instead of making a Melee attack. To do so, select the enemy as the subject of this attack and roll a D6. On a 1, nothing occurs and the model is destroyed. On a 2 to 4, the enemy unit suffers D3 Mortal Wounds, on a 5 or 6, the enemy unit suffers D6 Mortal Wounds. Models that use this ability are permanently destroyed and cannot be Resurrected using the Necrodermis ability.
