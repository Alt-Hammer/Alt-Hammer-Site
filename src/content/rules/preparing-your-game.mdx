## Force Organization

The army you play a game of Alt-Hammer with is comprised of Units. There is no limitation on the total number of Units your force can include, however, your army's force organization must abide by the following: 
1. Your army must include at least 1 Leader unit. 
2. 1 Leader unit in your army must be designated as your Warlord. Note that some Factions have Leader units that must be your Warlord if you take them, as described in their Unit Profile. Under a Force Organization section.
3. You can include a maximum of 2 of the same units in your army for every 1,000 points, unless that Unit either:
4. Possesses the Battleline keyword, in which case you can take up to 2 such units for every 500 points,
5. or
6. Possesses the Character or Titanic keywords, in which case you can take up to 1 such unit for every 1,000 points,
7. or
8. Possess the Transport [X] ability, in which case you can take 1 Transport [X] unit for every Infantry unit in your army,
9. or
10. Has Unit-specific conditions on the number that can be included in your army, as identified on its Unit Profile under a Force Organization section.

## Pregame Setup

Alt-Hammer games begin by determining the mission that will be played according to the Generating a Mission section, or by another mission design strategy agreed to by the players.
Once the mission is determined, the following step is completed, which commences the battle:
**Determine Player ****Initiative**** for Battle Round 1: **Each player rolls a D6. The player with the highest roll takes the Initiative for the first Battle Round. Having the Initiative means the following:
1. The player gets to complete the first unit Activation in Battle Round 1 
2. The order that players complete any Pre-battle Rules that apply to their army (ie Scout [X]" moves)
3. The order that the players complete the Command Phase

Please note that Initiative is determined separately for each Battle Round, as detailed in Phase 3 of The Battle Round.
