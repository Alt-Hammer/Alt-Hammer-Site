Alt-Hammer battles last for 5 Battle Rounds. A Battle Round is structured as follows:


## Phase 1: Command Phase


In the Command Phase, each player resolves the following activities, in the prescribed order, following the turn order as determined by the most recent Initiative Determination Phase:
- **Complete any Rally Checks** for units that became Battle Shocked in the prior Battle Round, as detailed in the Battle Shock section. Skip this step for Battle Round 1.
- **Acquire new Command Points**. Some units may grant the player additional Command Points. These Command Points are acquired at this time as well.
- **Generate Secondary Mission Objectives**, if you are using Secondary Mission Objectives in your battle. (Skip this step if you are not using Secondary Mission Objectives in your battle.)
- **Resolve any Command Phase abilities**. These may be your Faction's Army Rules, or special abilities your <span class="keyword" data-term="character" data-type="keyword">Character</span> units may have.
- **Tally up any Primary and Secondary Points achieved in the prior Battle Round** as per your Mission Objectives as applicable. Skip this step for Battle Round 1.

The Command Phase ends when each player has completed all of the above.


## Phase 2: Activation Phase


In the Activation Phase, players begin taking turns Activating units in their army in the order determined by the most recent Initiative Determination Phase. When a unit is Activated it must then expend up to the maximum number of Activation Points available to that unit on eligible Actions. 

The Activation Phase ends when both players have completed all Activations and Bonus Activations they can make this Battle Round. 

**Tip: Use Tokens for Activated Units.** It is recommended that players use a standard form of token to mark which units have been Activated to avoid re-activating previously activated units. A small paper circle with a checkmark on it could be an example of such a token!


## Phase 3: Determine the Initiative for the Next Battle Round


In this phase, each player rolls a D6. The player with the highest roll takes the initiative in the next Battle Round. The player with the initiative:
- Completes their Command Phase first
- Activates the first unit in the Activation Phase.
