Many units in Alt-Hammer have psychic abilities drawn from the powers of the Warp. These units always possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="psyker" data-type="keyword">Psyker</span> keyword, typically possess Special Abilities marked as **[Psychic]**, and can typically access Psychic-type Ranged attacks. 
Using any Psychic abilities or attacks requires first taking a Psychic Test, which follows these steps:
1. Each time a unit uses its **[Psychic]** ability or makes a Psychic attack, the controlling player first rolls 2D6. 
2. The specific **[Psychic]** ability will describe when that ability is used (i.e., during your Command Phase, or when the unit is Activated)
3. Psychic attacks are considered Ranged weapons, and thus a Psychic Test for making that attack is completed as part of a <span class="keyword" data-term="shoot" data-type="action">Shoot</span> Action.
4. If the result of the roll is higher than the model’s Leadership characteristic, the Psychic Test is passed, and the **[Psychic]** ability or Psychic attack can proceed as normal.
5. If the result of the roll is lower than the model’s Leadership characteristic, the Psychic Test is failed, and the **[Psychic]** ability or Psychic attack cannot be used.
6. If the result of the roll is either two 1s or two 6s (i.e., a total value of 2 or 12), the unit suffers Perils of the Warp and immediately takes 2 D3 Mortal Wounds.
7. If the roll was a 2, the **[Psychic]** ability or Psychic attack cannot be used.
8. If the roll was a 12, the **[Psychic]** ability or Psychic attack can be used, but must target the closest unit - friendly or enemy.
9. If the unit suffering Perils of the Warp is destroyed as a result of the Mortal Wounds inflicted, that unit is considered to have the <span class="keyword" data-term="deadly-demise-d31" data-type="keyword">Deadly Demise D3+1</span> ability.

Note that some faction rules or unit special abilities may impact the results of Psychic Tests, or create requirements for <span class="keyword" data-term="psykers" data-type="keyword">Psykers</span> to take additional Psychic Tests even when not making Psychic attacks or using **[Psychic]** abilities.
