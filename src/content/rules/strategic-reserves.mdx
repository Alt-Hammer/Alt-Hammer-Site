Strategic Reserves allows a contingent from your army to be withheld from the battlefield until a later Battle Round of your choosing. Some Actions and abilities also allow units on the battlefield to be placed into Strategic Reserves before the battle begins, and throughout the battle as well. 

The following sets out the restrictions and conditions for using Strategic Reserves.
1. Up to half of your army’s total points value can start the battle in Strategic Reserves.
2. Unless a unit’s Abilities state otherwise, units in Strategic Reserves cannot use their faction’s Army Rules or abilities that occur in the Command Phase of the Battle Round.
3. Strategic Reserves can only be Activated during the 2nd Battle Round onwards.
4. Units in Strategic Reserves must <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> as their first Activation.
5. To <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span>, place the unit on the tabletop in your Deployment Zone so that that the unit is wholly within 6” of a Deployment Edge, and at least 4” away from any enemy models.
6. Units that possess the <span class="keyword" data-term="deep-strike" data-type="keyword">Deep Strike</span> ability can instead <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> anywhere on the battlefield at least 9” away from any enemy models. 
7. Some Army Rules or unit Special Abilities may allow units to <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span> in other ways not detailed here. Those Army Rules or Faction Abilities rules supersede these core rules.
8. Some unit Special Abilities or wargear may add restrictions to how units can <span class="keyword" data-term="enter-the-battlefield-from-strategic-reserves" data-type="action">Enter the Battlefield from Strategic Reserves</span>. Such Special Abilities or wargear supersede these core rules as well as any Army Rules or unit Special Abilities as detailed above.
